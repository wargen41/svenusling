Visste du?

  • The role of Bart was intended for [Richard Pryor][/p?id=1790], but due to the controversial nature of [Pryor's][/p?id=1790] stand-up routines of the day and his background, [Mel Brooks][/p?id=1634] couldn't secure financing for the project with [Pryor][/p?id=1790] in that role. So [Pryor][/p?id=1790] was made a co-writer of the script, and [Cleavon Little][/p?id=Cleavon%20Little] played Bart. [Pryor][/p?id=1790] later got to star in a different western comedy - Adiós Amigo (1976).
  • Production began with [Gig Young][/p?id=Gig%20Young] as the Waco Kid. On the first day of shooting, the scene where the drunk Waco Kid hangs from a bunk asking if Bart is black, [Young][/p?id=Gig%20Young] revealed that he really was indeed drunk (he had had an alcohol problem for years) and proceeded to undergo a physical collapse on set. [Brooks][/p?id=1634] shut down production for a day and [Gene Wilder][/p?id=1321] flew cross country to take over the role. [Young][/p?id=Gig%20Young] later sued Warner Bros. for breach of contract.
  • Hedy Lamarr sued [Mel Brooks][/p?id=1634] over the use of the name Hedley Lamarr and settled out of court. Mel said he was flattered by this attention.
  • The original plan for the film was to have [Alan Arkin][/p?id=1010] to direct the film with [James Earl Jones][/p?id=1417] playing the role of Bart.
  • [Madeline Kahn][/p?id=1599] started working on the movie the day after being fired from the role of Agnes Gooch in Mame (1974). [Lucille Ball][/p?id=Lucille%20Ball] believed that [Kahn][/p?id=1599] got herself fired (by deliberately acting poorly) so that she could take the role of Lili Von Shtupp in this film, but still get paid for the Gooch role in "Mame", which by her contract wouldn't have happened if she'd merely quit.
  • [Dom DeLuise][/p?id=1247] has claimed that the role of the director of the film-within-a-film 'The French Mistake' was originally meant to be played by actor [Peter Sellers][/p?id=1751]. But after he endured an exhaustive four-hour audition, [Brooks][/p?id=1634] instead cast [DeLuise][/p?id=1247].
