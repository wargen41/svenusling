  • The baby's name, Pubert, was the name originally suggested (and rejected) for Pugsley by Charles Addams when he was asked by the producers of "Familjen Addams" (1964) to name the hitherto unnamed characters in his cartoons.
  • The character of Pubert Addams (a boy) was played by two twin girls, [Kaitlyn Hooper][/p?id=Kaitlyn%20Hooper] and [Kristen Hooper][/p?id=Kristen%20Hooper].
  • [Carol Kane][/p?id=Carol%20Kane], who plays Grandmama, is almost a year younger than [Anjelica Huston][/p?id=1045], who plays her daughter, Morticia Addams.
  • Adding more than just make up to the character, Morticia Addams is always lit separate from everyone else in a scene. Her lighting always consists of one beam of light across her eyes that gradually fades outward to add to her grim look.
  • [Nathan Lane][/p?id=1689] has a cameo as a police officer in this movie. He would later go on to portray Gomez Addams in an off-Broadway stage adaptation of The Addams Family.
