  • [Warren Oates][/p?id=1992] was very ill with flu and chronic emphysema during filming.
  • Despite the fact that they played characters that were enemies in this film, both [Roy Scheider][/p?id=1843] and [Malcolm McDowell][/p?id=1604] became very good friends while working on it. As [McDowell][/p?id=1604] would state at various science-fiction conventions, they enjoyed working together so much that they couldn't leave each other alone. When [Scheider][/p?id=1843] passed away, a very saddened [McDowell][/p?id=1604] was unable to attend his funeral due to a film project that he was working on and could not leave at the time, much to his dismay. As a result, he immediately ordered some flowers and sent a letter of condolence to [Scheider's][/p?id=1843] family.
  • One reason [Roy Scheider][/p?id=1843] made this film was so that he would be unavailable to get cast in [Jaws 3-D][/f?id=14409] (1983).
  • This movie and [Tough Enough][/f?id=Tough%20Enough] (1983) were the final theatrical feature films of actor [Warren Oates][/p?id=1992]. [Tough Enough][/f?id=Tough%20Enough] (1983), the last released film of actor [Warren Oates][/p?id=1992], was actually filmed before [Oates'][/p?id=1992] penultimately released picture, [Blue Thunder][/f?id=11069] (1983), but was first released stateside about a week after [Blue Thunder][/f?id=11069] (1983).
