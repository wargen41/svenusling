  • When asked if he had any fears about playing a gay man, [Heath Ledger][/p?id=1366] replied that he was not afraid of the role, only that he wasn't mature enough to do it justice.
  • In March 2006 [Randy Quaid][/p?id=1769] filed a lawsuit against Focus Features alleging that the company had misled him into thinking that [Brokeback Mountain][/f?id=11324] was a low budget, art-house film with no prospect of making money. He saw this as a ruse to get him to lower his salary. At the time of the lawsuit, the film had earned more than $160 million. [Quaid][/p?id=1769] dropped the lawsuit in May, seemingly after Focus agreed to pay him a bonus. Focus, however, denied that any such payment ever took place, and Focus spokeswoman Adriene Bowles was quoted as saying, "the circumstances of him dropping the suit are as mysterious as the circumstances under which he filed his claim."
  • [Heath Ledger][/p?id=1366] declined to go to the one month cowboy camp that had been organized as he had grown up on farms in Western Australia. [Jake Gyllenhaal][/p?id=1407] was required to attend, however, as he needed "roughing up".
  • [Heath Ledger][/p?id=1366] reckoned that the script for [Brokeback Mountain][/f?id=11324] was the best screenplay he'd ever read.
