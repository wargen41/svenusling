Visste du?

  • The fish-and-chip restaurant where Andy takes Gloria when they decide to "go posh" is called "In Cod We Trust".
  • In the UK, it sparked a brief resurgence of interest in brass band music, with the CD soundtrack selling 50,000 copies.
  • A portion of [Pete Postlethwaite's][/p?id=1735] speech at the end of the film was sampled by the British group, Chumbawamba, for the beginning of their 1997 hit "Tubthumping."
  • [Kate Winslet][/p?id=1530] and [Catherine Zeta-Jones][/p?id=Catherine%20Zeta-Jones] were both considered for the part of Gloria.
  • [Pete Postlethwaite's][/p?id=1735] final speech was not rehearsed in front of the rest of the cast. This was because the director wanted genuine reactions from everyone on hearing it.
