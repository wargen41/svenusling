  • [Julie Delpy][/p?id=Julie%20Delpy] and [Kate Beckinsale][/p?id=1528] were two of the original people thought of for the role of Sophie. [Delpy][/p?id=Julie%20Delpy] wanted the role badly and lobbied for it, but she was ultimately turned down.
  • [Ron Howard][/p?id=Ron%20Howard] always wanted [Audrey Tautou][/p?id=Audrey%20Tautou] for the role of Sophie, but she was never available for an audition. She also originally felt too young to play opposite [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950]. After some convincing from [Howard][/p?id=Ron%20Howard] she auditioned. Other actors that auditioned for the role: [Sophie Marceau][/p?id=Sophie%20Marceau], [Virginie Ledoyen][/p?id=Virginie%20Ledoyen], [Judith Godrèche][/p?id=Judith%20Godr%C3%A8che], and [Linda Hardy][/p?id=Linda%20Hardy].
  • [Ron Howard's][/p?id=Ron%20Howard] first envisioned choice for the role of Robert Langdon was [Bill Paxton][/p?id=1083]. [Paxton][/p?id=1083] was interested but turned it down because of scheduling conflicts. [Russell Crowe][/p?id=1846] was then seriously considered for the role but ultimately [Howard][/p?id=Ron%20Howard] decided on his long-time friend [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] for the role. Other actors considered for the part were [Ralph Fiennes][/p?id=1767], [Hugh Jackman][/p?id=1379] and [George Clooney][/p?id=1325].
  • [Audrey Tautou][/p?id=Audrey%20Tautou] revealed that, during her audition, she asked if she could take a photo of [Ron Howard][/p?id=Ron%20Howard] and [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] to prove that she's actually met them.
