Visste du?

  • [Sylvester Stallone][/p?id=1915] gained 40 pounds to add realism to his portrayal of the police officer. He also was only paid SAG minimum wages. By the time that reshoots were required, he had lost the weight again.
  • [Sylvester Stallone][/p?id=1915] gained the necessary weight for the role by eating a steady diet of giant pancakes served at a local pancake house.
  • [Peter Berg][/p?id=Peter%20Berg] plays Officer Randone. In [Very Bad Things][/f?id=21297] (1998), directed by [Berg][/p?id=Peter%20Berg], the same character appears, played by [Steve Fitchpatrick][/p?id=Steve%20Fitchpatrick].
  • At the end of the film, when Freddy and Gary are bringing in Murray Babitch to the NYC authorities, there is one shot of Moe Tilden coming out of a building. In this one shot, his mustache is noticeably longer. Director [James Mangold][/p?id=James%20Mangold] wanted to get a shot of [Robert De Niro's][/p?id=1808] character walking out of the building. [De Niro][/p?id=1808] had already completed his scenes and was already working on another film, [Jackie Brown][/f?id=15186] (1997). [De Niro][/p?id=1808] agreed to return and appear in the shot, but could not trim his longer mustache as it was required for his character, Louis Gara, in [Jackie Brown][/f?id=15186] (1997).
  • [Deborah Harry][/p?id=Deborah%20Harry] acted in the movie but was edited out in the final cut. She explained on a live television special that although she was cut, she still got paid.
  • All the actors in the film worked for scale due to the film's budget.
  • In the scene in which [Ray Liotta][/p?id=1771] confronts [Robert Patrick][/p?id=1818] in the bar, the dart that [Liotta][/p?id=1771] shoves up [Patrick's][/p?id=1818] left nostril was made out of rubber.
  • [Robert Patrick][/p?id=1818] got the part after running into actor [Peter Berg][/p?id=Peter%20Berg] and the film's producer and music supervisor, Meryl Poster, who were staying in the same hotel in New York. He got the script and then read for director [James Mangold][/p?id=James%20Mangold] a few days later.
  • The sandwich scene in [Robert De Niro's][/p?id=1808] office was [Stallone's][/p?id=1915] first day shooting. The sandwiches, which were an afterthought for the actors' lunch, were improvised into the scene by [De Niro][/p?id=1808].
  • [John Travolta][/p?id=1501], [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950], and [Tom Cruise][/p?id=1949] where among the actors that were considered for the role of Sheriff Freddy Heflin.
  • Originally, [Ray Liotta][/p?id=1771] wanted the role of Sheriff Freddy Heflin, while [Sylvester Stallone][/p?id=1915] wanted the role of Gary "Figgsy" Figgis.
