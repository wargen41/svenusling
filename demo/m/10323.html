Visste du?

  • [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] suggested [Chazz Palminteri][/p?id=Chazz%20Palminteri] for the role of Primo Sidone, having worked together on [A Bronx Tale][/f?id=15008] (1993).
  • [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] was first approached to direct the film but turned it down.
  • The character of Paul Vitti was loosely inspired by real life mobster John Gotti.
  • [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] picked out the shirt collars used by Vitti and his crew.
  • It was very important to [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] that the movie felt authentic. He and director [Harold Ramis][/p?id=1356] would drive around Little Italy in New York looking for real people to cast for the movie.
  • According to [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808], he and [Billy Crystal][/p?id=1087] briefly toyed with the idea of co-directing the movie before deciding to offer it to [Harold Ramis][/p?id=1356], who accepted.
