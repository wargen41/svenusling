Visste du?

  • [Tom Hardy][/p?id=Tom%20Hardy] was approached for the title role of Lincoln, but had to turn it down due to scheduling conflicts with [The Dark Knight][/f?id=11809] Rises. Later, [Eric Bana][/p?id=Eric%20Bana], [Timothy Olyphant][/p?id=Timothy%20Olyphant], [Adrien Brody][/p?id=1004], [Josh Lucas][/p?id=Josh%20Lucas], [James D'Arcy][/p?id=James%20D'Arcy] and Oliver Jackson-Cohen were considered to play the role before [Benjamin Walker][/p?id=Benjamin%20Walker] was cast.
  • [Joaquin Phoenix][/p?id=1466] was the first choice for the role of Henry Sturgess, but turned it down.
  • Robin McLeavy was considered to play Lincoln's wife Mary Todd, and was later cast as Lincoln's mother Nancy.
  • [Trent Reznor][/p?id=Trent%20Reznor] was approached about scoring and playing a minor part in the film, but the news leaked before the final decision and [Reznor][/p?id=Trent%20Reznor] voted to pass on the project as the surprise element of his participation had already been lost.
  • Joshua Speed (played by [Jimmi Simpson][/p?id=Jimmi%20Simpson]) was one of Abraham Lincoln's oldest friends dating back to their days in Springfield Illinois. Although Joshua never actually came to Washington his brother James Speed was considered one of Lincoln's oldest friends in Washington and served as Attorney General from late 1864 until he resigned in 1866.
