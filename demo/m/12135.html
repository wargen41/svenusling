  • As always with the James Bond series, several rumors anticipated the making of this movie. Some said that former American president [Bill Clinton][/p?id=Bill%20Clinton] would play the part of an American politician, and that all the movie would be shot in Ireland, as a kind of tribute to [Pierce Brosnan's][/p?id=1760] homeland. In these rumors, the plot would be about the kidnapping of the British Prime Minister in Dublin, and the villain would be an American played by [Kevin Spacey][/p?id=1550]. Of course, none of this gossip were proved true. Published reports in 2001 indicated that [Whitney Houston][/p?id=Whitney%20Houston] was being considered for the role of Jinx in this film. At the pre-production stage, [Saffron Burrows][/p?id=Saffron%20Burrows] and [Salma Hayek][/p?id=1850] were both considered for roles. It was also rumored that [Billy Connolly][/p?id=1086] was asked to play the part of a villain in the teaser sequence, but turned it down. According to television news reports on 11 November 2002, [Sean Connery][/p?id=1866] filmed a cameo as James Bond's father. However, this has been denied by producers [Michael G. Wilson][/p?id=Michael%20G.%20Wilson] and Barbara Broccoli, who later said on record it would be pointless to spend the money and effort to get [Connery][/p?id=1866] and then not use his scene. Some other rumors said that the movie would be called "Beyond the Ice" or "Final Assignment". Despite that, one rumor that linked director [Brett Ratner][/p?id=Brett%20Ratner] to the production was true, but the producers preferred a non-American director.
  • A knee injury to [Pierce Brosnan][/p?id=1760] delayed shooting for a few weeks.
  • [Halle Berry's][/p?id=Halle%20Berry] bikini scenes were shot in Cadiz and were not sunny and warm as they appeared onscreen but quite the opposite. [Berry][/p?id=Halle%20Berry] had to be wrapped up in thick towels in between takes to avoid catching a chill.
  • Debris from a smoke grenade landed in [Halle Berry's][/p?id=Halle%20Berry] eye during filming. The actress required a 30 minute operation to remove it.
  • [Roger Moore][/p?id=1835] actively voiced his displeasure with the film, citing the invisible car and the weak CGI as being a low for the series.
  • [Pierce Brosnan's][/p?id=1760] last appearance as James Bond. The role was taken over in 2006 by [Daniel Craig][/p?id=1203] for [Casino Royale (2006)][/f?id=11458].
  • After the release of this movie, [Pierce Brosnan][/p?id=1760] was approached by a man in a Dublin bar who asked to shake his hand. [Brosnan][/p?id=1760] complied and then cracked up when the man quipped, "That's the closest my hand will ever get to [Halle Berry's][/p?id=Halle%20Berry] arse".
