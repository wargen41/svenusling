Visste du?

  • [Emilio Estevez][/p?id=1278] was originally going to play Bender, but [Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] couldn't find someone to play Andrew Clark so [Emilio][/p?id=1278] agreed to play Clark.
  • [Molly Ringwald][/p?id=Molly%20Ringwald] really wanted to play Allison but [Ally Sheedy][/p?id=Ally%20Sheedy] had already been promised the part.
  • [Anthony Michael Hall's][/p?id=Anthony%20Michael%20Hall] mother and younger sister play his character Brian's mother and sister in the movie.
  • Director [John Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] insisted that the entire cast and crew eat their meals on location in the Maine North High School cafeteria.
  • The joke that Bender tells but never finishes (while crawling through the ceiling) actually has no punchline. According to [Judd Nelson][/p?id=1518], he ad-libbed the line. Originally, he was supposed to tell a joke that would end when he came back into the library and said, "Forgot my pencil", but no one could come up with a joke for that punchline.
  • [Nicolas Cage][/p?id=1696] was originally considered for the role of John Bender but the production couldn't afford his salary at the time. [John Cusack][/p?id=1480] was originally cast as John Bender, but [John Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] decided to replace him with [Judd Nelson][/p?id=1518] before shooting began.
  • Director [John Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] said that the cast rehearsed the entire movie as if it was a play a few times before filming began. After the film was a hit, [Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] was asked to write the script as a play so high schoolers could perform it.
  • [Rick Moranis][/p?id=1795] was originally cast as the janitor; he left due to creative differences and was replaced by [John Kapelos][/p?id=John%20Kapelos].
  • The scene in which all characters sit in a circle on the floor in the library and tell stories about why they were in detention was not scripted. [John Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] told them all to ad-lib.
  • [John Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] almost fired [Judd Nelson][/p?id=1518] because of his negative attitude towards [Molly Ringwald][/p?id=Molly%20Ringwald] off camera. [Paul Gleason][/p?id=Paul%20Gleason] convinced [Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] that [Nelson][/p?id=1518] was a great actor and was merely trying to stay in character.
  • It was originally suggested that there would be several sequels to The [Breakfast Club][/f?id=11301] (1985), occurring every ten years, in which "The [Breakfast Club][/f?id=11301]" would get back together.
  • Shot entirely in sequence.
  • Any hope of a sequel to "The [Breakfast Club][/f?id=11301]" was basically dashed due to the volatile relationship between [John Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] and [Judd Nelson][/p?id=1518]. [John Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] has stated that he would never work with [Nelson][/p?id=1518] again. Also, it is unclear whether or not [Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes] still holds ill-will against his oft-cast starlet, [Molly Ringwald][/p?id=Molly%20Ringwald]. They had a falling out in the late eighties after [Ringwald][/p?id=Molly%20Ringwald] decided to move on from the teen film genre to pursue more adult roles, thus severing her relationship with [Hughes][/p?id=John%20Hughes].
  • At the time of shooting, [Molly Ringwald][/p?id=Molly%20Ringwald] and [Anthony Michael Hall][/p?id=Anthony%20Michael%20Hall] were the only [Breakfast Club][/f?id=11301] members of high school age, both being 16. [Emilio Estevez][/p?id=1278] and [Ally Sheedy][/p?id=Ally%20Sheedy] both were 22, and [Judd Nelson][/p?id=1518] was 25.
  • [Jodie Foster][/p?id=1467] was considered to play Claire and [Brooke Shields][/p?id=Brooke%20Shields] was considered to play Allison respectively.
