Visste du?

  • [Nicolas Cage][/p?id=1696] had two teeth removed (without anesthetic) for this role.
  • Then-unknown [Danny Glover][/p?id=1208] was scheduled to make a speech as a bit player, but because he kept botching his lines his scenes had to be cut from the movie.
  • First use of the skycam in a feature film. It was a camera held by wires supported by four cranes and controlled by computer, and was used in Birdy's dream sequence of actually being able to fly.
  • [Matthew Modine][/p?id=Matthew%20Modine] auditioned for the role of Al but [Alan Parker][/p?id=Alan%20Parker] cast him as Birdy instead.
  • Birds who acted in the film had their own bird billing section in the movie's closing credits.
  • Film debut of [Marshall Bell][/p?id=Marshall%20Bell].
