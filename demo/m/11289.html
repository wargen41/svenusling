  • According to both [David Carradine][/p?id=1213] and [Barbara Hershey][/p?id=Barbara%20Hershey], their sex scene was not faked.
  • This was [Martin Scorsese's][/p?id=1619] first feature film. He was given $600,000 and told to make an exploitation film.
  • After he finished this film, [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] screened the film for [John Cassavetes][/p?id=John%20Cassavetes]. [Cassavetes][/p?id=John%20Cassavetes], after seeing this film, hugged [Scorsese][/p?id=1619] and said, "Martin, you just spent a year of your life making shit!" He urged him to make a personal film, and the result was [Mean Streets][/f?id=13294] (1973).
  • [Roger Corman's][/p?id=Roger%20Corman] wife Julie obtained the film rights to the story from Bertha Thompson herself who she tracked down in a San Francisco hotel room but never actually met since she wouldn't open the door.
