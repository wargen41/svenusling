Visste du?

  • Hollywood, California, Monday, October 17, 1938: [Darryl F. Zanuck][/p?id=Darryl%20F.%20Zanuck] has selected [Sidney Toler][/p?id=Sidney%20Toler] to play the role of Charlie Chan, succeeding the late [Warner Oland][/p?id=1990]. His first picture will be "Charlie Chan in Honolulu" which will start production October 24, with [John Stone][/p?id=John%20Stone] as the associate producer. [Toler][/p?id=Sidney%20Toler] was discovered by Sol M. Wurtzel when he looked at rushes of Up the River, current 20th Century-Fox picture in which [Toler][/p?id=Sidney%20Toler] is a featured player.
  • Among other actors considered and tested for the new Charlie Chan were [Cy Kendall][/p?id=Cy%20Kendall], [Walter Connolly][/p?id=Walter%20Connolly], [J. Edward Bromberg][/p?id=J.%20Edward%20Bromberg], [Noah Beery Jr.][/p?id=Noah%20Beery%20Jr.], [Michael Visaroff][/p?id=Michael%20Visaroff], and [Leo Carillo][/p?id=Leo%20Carillo]. [Kendall][/p?id=Cy%20Kendall] and Connelly had played Chan on radio.
