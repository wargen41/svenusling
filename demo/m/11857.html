Visste du?

  • The blond wigs that the children wear had a built-in dome to give the impression that they had a larger than normal cranium.
  • Originally begun in 1957 as an American picture to star [Ronald Colman][/p?id=Ronald%20Colman], MGM shelved the project, because it was deemed potentially inflammatory and controversial, specifically due to its sinister depiction of virgin birth.
  • [Ronald Colman][/p?id=Ronald%20Colman] was originally supposed to star in this film. He passed away in 1958 and was replaced by [George Sanders][/p?id=1328], who had married [Colman's][/p?id=Ronald%20Colman] widow Benita Hume in 1959.
