Visste du?

  • Near the end of the car chase, Doug originally said "Jesus" after first seeing Oerstadt's face. This caused the audience at the first preview screening to erupt into laughter, as [James Caviezel][/p?id=James%20Caviezel], who played Oerstadt, is famous for playing Jesus in [The Passion Of The Christ][/f?id=17380] (2004). This unintentional reference had gone overlooked by director [Tony Scott][/p?id=Tony%20Scott]; he had the line removed from the film before its release.
  • The first [Tony Scott][/p?id=Tony%20Scott] film to be rated PG-13 since [Days Of Thunder][/f?id=11846] (1990).
  • [James Caviezel][/p?id=James%20Caviezel] decided at the last minute to do the stunt where he is hit by a car himself. If injured he could have been paralyzed.
