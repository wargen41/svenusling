  • Expanded from a Canadian TV production Arthur Hailey wrote called Flight Into Danger, whose cast included Canadian actor and future [Star Trek][/f?id=19154] engineer [James Doohan][/p?id=James%20Doohan].
  • At the request of [Dean Martin][/p?id=1231], [Petula Clark][/p?id=Petula%20Clark] was originally offered the role of Gwen Meighen.
  • [Dean Martin][/p?id=1231] received 10% of the film's gross, which added an additional $7,000,000 to his salary.
  • [Burt Lancaster][/p?id=1120], who headlined the film above the title with [Dean Martin][/p?id=1231], made a great deal of money from the film, which was a huge hit. [Lancaster's][/p?id=1120] contract gave him a 10% profit participation once the movie hit $50 million, and the film grossed $45.3 million in North America alone. Despite the financial windfall, [Lancaster][/p?id=1120] said that the movie was "the worst piece of junk ever made."
