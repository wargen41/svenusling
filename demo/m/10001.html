  • Murdock ([Sharlto Copley][/p?id=Sharlto%20Copley]) pretends to be a cameraman for the South African Broadcasting Corporation while getting items for a mission. [Copley][/p?id=Sharlto%20Copley] is a native of South Africa.
  • B.A. Baracus bears the tattoos "Pity" and "Fool" on his knuckles, as a tribute to [Mr. T's][/p?id=Mr.%20T] catchphrase from "[Rocky III][/f?id=17744]": "I pity the fool!". Contrary to popular myth, B.A. Baracus never uttered that exact phrase on "[The][/p?id=Mr.%20T] A-[Team][/p?id=Mr.%20T]".
  • [Sharlto Copley's][/p?id=Sharlto%20Copley] mom banned him from watching the [TV][/p?id=Mr.%20T] show, even though he watched it at friend's houses, due to its violent nature. [The][/p?id=Mr.%20T] first thing he did when he got the role of Murdock was phone his mom and told her "that I was just getting her back for not letting me watch the series when I was a kid."
  • Hannibal Smith is famous for smoking cigars. [Liam Neeson][/p?id=1584], who plays Hannibal, is an ex-smoker.

  • [Bradley Cooper][/p?id=1098] became so efficient in weapons training that he was able to reload his M4 rifle in less than 4 seconds. [This][/p?id=Mr.%20T] was actually in the film but was left on the cutting room floor.
