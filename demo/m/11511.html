Visste du?

  • Stage manager [Maurice Cass][/p?id=Maurice%20Cass] vows that the opera will go on "even if Frankenstein walks in!" Audiences were well aware that this in-joke referred to star [Boris Karloff][/p?id=1095], who was in the theater at the time.
  • The unique billing listed [Warner Oland][/p?id=1990] vs. [Boris Karloff][/p?id=1095] above the title. [Karloff][/p?id=1095] had turned down the title role in ::Dr Yogami Från London||Werewolf of London, which would have pitted him against his current co-star. The part was ultimately played by [Henry Hull][/p?id=Henry%20Hull].
  • [Boris Karloff][/p?id=1095], did not do his own singing in this film. His voice was dubbed by a singer named [Tudor Williams][/p?id=Tudor%20Williams].
  • Though only excerpts are used, Oscar Levant wrote an entire short opera for this film.
