Visste du?

  • [Mike Myers's][/p?id=1674] entrance as Goldmember, owner of Club 69, parodies his earlier role as the owner of Studio 54 in the movie [54][/f?id=54] (1998).
  • Director [Jay Roach's][/p?id=Jay%20Roach] first cut of the film ran 3 hours long.
  • [Sean Connery][/p?id=1866] was considered to play Nigel Powers.
  • The number 42 appears at least two times in this film: - in the prison scene, Mini Me and Dr. Evil wear jerseys with the number on it - in the submarine scene, it appears on the accident sign. At the time, director [Jay Roach][/p?id=Jay%20Roach] was set to direct the movie of [Douglas Adams][/p?id=Douglas%20Adams] [The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy][/f?id=15743] (2005), which features significance to the number 42.
  • [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] is wearing a cap with the Precrime logo, which comes from his own film [Minority Report][/f?id=16200] (2002).
  • [Mike Myers][/p?id=1674] has said that his glasses in all three films are meant to look like those worn by [Michael Caine][/p?id=1643] in [The Ipcress File][/f?id=13549] (1965), so [Caine][/p?id=1643] took the original glasses (model "Teviot 74" by UK Optical) to the set and wore them as Nigel.
  • [Michael York][/p?id=1667] and [Michael Caine][/p?id=1643], both veteran English actors with over 100 films each to his credit, had never worked on a film together before this one.
