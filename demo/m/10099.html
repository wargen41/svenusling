Visste du?

  • When work began on the movie in 1993, [Joseph Mazzello][/p?id=Joseph%20Mazzello] was cast as David.
  • [Haley Joel Osment][/p?id=Haley%20Joel%20Osment] suggested to [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] that his character (David) should not blink. [Spielberg][/p?id=1907] agreed and went further to suggest that none of the androids should blink. In fact, several of them do.
  • One of the buildings in Manhattan is actually an Apple Macintosh (Harman-Kardon) subwoofer.
  • [John Williams][/p?id=John%20Williams] quotes Richard Strauss' "Der Rosenkavalier" waltz in the underscore during the approach Rouge City. This was a done to honor [Stanley Kubrick][/p?id=1891], who left few notes regarding the music except to tell [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] that this Strauss waltz should appear in the film. Williams refers to his score, which contains a number of musical allusions to [Kubrick's][/p?id=1891] films in addition to the waltz, as his 'homage a [Kubrick'][/p?id=1891].
  • [Steven Spielberg's][/p?id=1907] writing credit is his first since [Poltergeist][/f?id=Poltergeist] (1982).
  • One of the reasons for [Stanley Kubrick][/p?id=1891] waiting so long to make the film, is that he wanted David ([Haley Joel Osment][/p?id=Haley%20Joel%20Osment]) to be played by an actual robot. After [Jurassic Park][/f?id=15341] (1993) was made, [Kubrick][/p?id=1891] looked into using digital computer effects to create David.
  • Real-life amputees played some of the robots with missing limbs.
  • [Julianne Moore][/p?id=1524] and [Gwyneth Paltrow][/p?id=1349] were considered for the role of Monica.
  • [Stanley Kubrick][/p?id=1891] worked on the project for 12 years before his death, but along the way decided to let [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] direct, saying it was "closer to his sensibilities". The two collaborated for years, resulting in [Kubrick][/p?id=1891] giving [Spielberg][/p?id=1907] a complete treatment and lots of conceptual art for the film prior to his death.
  • [Robin Williams][/p?id=1828] actually recorded his dialog for the film with [Stanley Kubrick][/p?id=1891] directing the recording session, he did it a long time before [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] was attached to direct.
  • [Jack Angel][/p?id=Jack%20Angel], [Robin Williams][/p?id=1828], [Meryl Streep][/p?id=1641], [Ben Kingsley][/p?id=1074] and [Chris Rock][/p?id=1333], all of whom voiced their characters, were all cast by [Stanley Kubrick][/p?id=1891] and recorded their dialogue under his direction.
