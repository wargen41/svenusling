Visste du?

  • Was inspired by a biography show that [Will Ferrell][/p?id=2000] watched about [Jessica Savitch][/p?id=Jessica%20Savitch], and how one of her male coworkers confessed to being a total chauvinist back in the day.
  • The first draft of the screenplay included suggested actors for various roles: - Champ Kind: [John C. Reilly][/p?id=1474] - Brick Tamland: [Chris Parnell][/p?id=Chris%20Parnell] - Brian Fantana: [Ben Stiller][/p?id=1075] - Ed Harken: [Ed Harris][/p?id=1267] - Garth Holiday: [Dan Aykroyd][/p?id=1196] - Frank Vitchard: [Alec Baldwin][/p?id=1017] - The script also specified another member of the news team, Marshall Connors, with [William H. Macy][/p?id=2004] suggested for the part.
  • [Adam McKay][/p?id=Adam%20McKay] has said that in the first draft of the script, the story was about a planeload of news anchors who crash in the mountains and discover that the plane which they collided was carrying monkeys and martial arts equipment, leading to a battle between cannibalistic newsmen and star-throwing monkeys.
  • This film had been pitched to DreamWorks nearly 20 times before, following the successes of [Old School][/f?id=17250] (2003) and Elf (2003). DreamWorks had little faith in the film, doubting [Will Ferrell][/p?id=2000] and [Adam McKay][/p?id=Adam%20McKay] were able to pull off an entire film based on news anchors. Despite the doubts the film would bomb, it grossed $84 million domestically and the director had so much extra footage they were able to make a second film out of it.
  • [Amy Poehler][/p?id=Amy%20Poehler] was completely cut out of the movie.
  • [Maggie Gyllenhaal][/p?id=1600] auditioned for the role of Veronica.
  • [Paul Thomas][/p?id=Paul%20Thomas] Anderson was originally a producer on the project.
