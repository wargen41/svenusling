  • The first film by [Kevin Smith][/p?id=1549] to not be written by him, as well as his first produced by a major studio.
  • Warner Bros. picked up the project in turnaround in 2008 from Gold Circle Films, where it had been in development with [Robin Williams][/p?id=1828] and [James Gandolfini][/p?id=1420] in the lead roles.
  • [Jason Mewes][/p?id=Jason%20Mewes] and [Ben Affleck][/p?id=1070] were approached for roles in this film, but couldn't make it due to scheduling conflicts.
  • [Kevin Smith][/p?id=1549] personally thought of Harold Faltermeyer to compose the score, feeling that the film was an homage to the '80s cop comedy genre, and needed one of the genre's most prominent composers. Faltermeyer was previously in retirement, but was looking to come back, and after seeing a rough cut of the film, signed on.
