  • A day after the terrorist attacks on the USA of 11 September 2001, [Roger Michell][/p?id=Roger%20Michell] had the WTC towers digitally removed from the opening main title sequence in the film. In the DVD commentary, he admitted that it was a mistake to erase them and make it appear as if they did not exist. During the re-editing of the film, [Michell][/p?id=Roger%20Michell] reinserted them as a tribute.
  • [Roger Michell][/p?id=Roger%20Michell] actually attended Alcoholic's Anonymous meetings in order to get the idea of how Doyle Gipson's ([Samuel L. Jackson][/p?id=1856]) A.A. meetings would play out on screen.
  • [Ben Affleck][/p?id=1070] was called for re-shoots on [Pearl Harbor][/f?id=17400] (2001) during principal photography on this movie.
