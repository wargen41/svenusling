Visste du?

  • In the Las Vegas bar scene where Austin flashes the peace sign and is laughed at, [Mike Myers][/p?id=1674] originally wanted Austin to be drinking a Zima to show Powers desperately trying to be cool and failing (in the movie he's drinking a Tab cola). The Zima company was keen on the idea until they realized they were being mocked, and quickly pulled out.
  • Both [Elizabeth Hurley][/p?id=Elizabeth%20Hurley] and [Mimi Rogers][/p?id=Mimi%20Rogers] asked to keep their tight leather outfits and were allowed to.
  • Part of the influence for this film can be seen in an early sketch [Mike Myers][/p?id=1674] did for "Saturday Night Live" (1975) in which several of James Bond's nemeses complain about his invulnerability and how much easier it should be to kill him.
  • The evil, shoe-throwing henchman "Random Task" is based on the character "Oddjob" from the James Bond film [Goldfinger][/f?id=14302] (1964). In addition to the play on his name, Oddjob also threw deadly hats.
  • [Myers][/p?id=1674] said that he based the [Austin Powers][/f?id=10510] partially on [Michael Caine's][/p?id=1643] character in [Alfie][/f?id=13547] (1966).
  • Comedian [Colin Quinn][/p?id=Colin%20Quinn] was offered the role of Scott Evil, but he turned it down to focus on his writing projects.
