  • [Emma Stone][/p?id=1281] personally wrote Gwen's graduation speech.
  • [Shailene Woodley][/p?id=Shailene%20Woodley] was cast as Mary-[Jane Watson][/p?id=Jane%20Watson], and even filmed scenes. But her role was cut from the film because the filmmakers felt there were too many characters and wanted to streamline the series.
  • The humorously hateful news boss J. Jonah Jameson, one of the most iconic characters in [Spider-Man's][/f?id=18668] mythos, was never mentioned in The Amazing [Spider-Man][/f?id=18668] (2012). In this sequel he is spoken of frequently but never appears. The producers claimed they could not find an actor to measure up to [J.K. Simmons'][/p?id=J.K.%20Simmons] legendary portrayal of this character in [Sam Raimi's][/p?id=Sam%20Raimi] [Spider-Man][/f?id=18668] films. [Stan Lee][/p?id=Stan%20Lee], the writer who came up with the idea for these characters and makes cameo appearances in all the films, has often said he would volunteer to play Jameson if he ([Lee][/p?id=Stan%20Lee]) were 30 or 40 years younger.
