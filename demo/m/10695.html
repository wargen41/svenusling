  • While learning to twirl a cane, [Jim Carrey][/p?id=1460] reportedly broke around a dozen prop canes and some of his trailer furniture.
  • In the film there is a doctor named [Burton][/p?id=1937]. [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] directed the first two [Batman][/f?id=10692] films and produced this sequel.
  • At one moment, to [Batman's][/f?id=10692] surprise, Robin exclaims: "Holey rusted metal, [Batman][/f?id=10692]!", then explaining: "The ground, it's all metal. It's full of holes. You know, holey" - a jokey reference to the TV show "[Batman][/f?id=10691]" (1966) with [Adam West][/p?id=Adam%20West], where Robin would yell his trademark exclamations of surprise every once in a while: "Holy something-or-other, [Batman][/f?id=10692]!".
  • [Rene Russo][/p?id=1775] was originally cast to play Dr. Chase Meridian when [Michael Keaton][/p?id=1655] was still attached to the project as [Batman][/f?id=10692]. However, when [Keaton][/p?id=1655] dropped out of the project and was replaced by [Val Kilmer][/p?id=1973], [Russo][/p?id=1775] was deemed too old to play his love interest and was replaced by [Nicole Kidman][/p?id=1697].
  • [William Baldwin][/p?id=William%20Baldwin] was reported to be considered for the role of [Batman][/f?id=10692].
  • In the original [Batman][/f?id=10692] (1989), District Attorney [Harvey][/f?id=Harvey] Dent was played by [Billy Dee Williams][/p?id=Billy%20Dee%20Williams]. [Williams][/p?id=1828] accepted the role with the knowledge and expectation that Dent would eventually become Two-Face: he reportedly had a clause put into his contract reserving the role for him in any sequels, which Warner Bros. had to buy out so they could cast [Tommy Lee Jones][/p?id=1957].
  • According to [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937], he originally felt very enthusiastic about the idea of directing a third [Batman][/f?id=10692] film. However, when Warner Brothers executives expressed concern over the direction of the franchise - namely that [Batman][/f?id=10692] Returns (1992) had been too dark to appeal to a family audience - [Burton][/p?id=1937] decided it best to step down as director.
  • While [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] was still slated to direct the film, [Robin Williams][/p?id=1828] was running to play The Riddler, but after [Burton][/p?id=1937] dropped out, [Williams][/p?id=1828] lost interest and turned down the role despite being offered.
  • [Christian Bale][/p?id=1163] had auditioned for the role of Robin. Ironically in the next few years, he went on to star as Bruce Wayne/[Batman][/f?id=10692] in [Batman Begins][/f?id=10694] (2005) and its sequel [The Dark Knight][/f?id=11809] (2008).
  • Scheduling conflicts with [Braveheart][/f?id=11298] (1995) forced [Mel Gibson][/p?id=1635] to turn down the role of Harvey Dent/Two-Face.
  • [Jim Carrey][/p?id=1460] was paid $7 million to play the Riddler.
  • [Michelle Pfeiffer][/p?id=1668] was considered to reprise her role as [Catwoman][/f?id=11473].
