  • [Sigourney Weaver][/p?id=1882] was originally cast in the film, but had to drop out due to a conflicting schedule.

  • The Mock Turtle is portrayed as a real turtle in this series. However, in the "Alice in Wonderland" novel, the Mock Turtle had a turtle shell, but with limbs from different livestock. This was because mock turtle soup (for those who couldn't afford to have real turtle soup) is made from an assortment of different animals (poultry, beef, etc). This complex joke was erased, or more likely omitted, from the story.
