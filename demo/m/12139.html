Visste du?

  • While in early development with a script that was eventually discarded, the movie had been given the subtitle "Tears of the Sun". [Bruce Willis][/p?id=1117] told the studio he would commit to a Die Hard 4 if he could use the title for [Tears Of The Sun][/f?id=20061] (2003).
  • This film addresses the apparent continuity error in earlier installments - McClane is afraid of flying in the first two films, but not the third. Here, he explains that he took flying lessons in order to "face his fears."
  • [Justin Timberlake][/p?id=Justin%20Timberlake] was in talks to play John McClane's son.
  • In the beginning credits when [Kevin Smith's][/p?id=1549] name comes on the screen. The "m" in smith disappears and you see "Sith" for a few seconds paying homage to [Kevin Smith's][/p?id=1549] love of all things [Stjärnornas Krig][/f?id=19851] (1977) which also reflects in his character in the movie.
  • [Kevin Smith][/p?id=1549] rewrote the lines for his [Warlock][/f?id=21885] character. [Bruce Willis][/p?id=1117] thought the rewrite was too funny and did not follow the serious mood of the movie. [Smith][/p?id=1549] then rewrote the part to what [Willis][/p?id=1117] requested.
