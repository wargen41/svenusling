  • The first instance of a female star directing herself.
  • This is the only time [Ida Lupino][/p?id=Ida%20Lupino] ever directed herself.
  • There are several in-jokes about Santa Claus and Kris Kringle in the film, all at the expense of co-star [Edmund Gwenn][/p?id=1263] who played Kringle in the film [][/f?id=12047]Miracle[ on 34th Street][/f?id=%20on%2034th%20Street] (1947).
  • During the Beverly Hills tour of movie star homes, the houses the driver says belong to [Jack Benny][/p?id=Jack%20Benny] and [James Stewart][/p?id=1425] are their actual homes on Roxbury Drive.
  • This would be the last feature film directed by [Ida Lupino][/p?id=Ida%20Lupino] for more than 12 years until [The Trouble with Angels][/f?id=The%20Trouble%20with%20Angels] (1966).

  • Originally set up as an RKO production, it fell into difficulties when RKO pulled out, leaving the film without distribution.
  • Jane Wagner was originally set to play one of the wives but backed out at the last minute. [Joan Fontaine][/p?id=1464] took on the role instead.
