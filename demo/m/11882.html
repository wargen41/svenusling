Visste du?

  • This is [Gene Hackman's][/p?id=1317] and [Dustin Hoffman's][/p?id=1254] first film together. At the Pasadena Playhouse they were classmates and were both voted "Least Likely to Succeed".
  • This is the 10th movie starring both [John Cusack][/p?id=1480] and [Jeremy Piven][/p?id=Jeremy%20Piven].
  • After being in development for several years, director [Alfonso Cuarón][/p?id=Alfonso%20Cuar%C3%B3n] became attached to direct and co-write the film. He ended up leaving the project a few months after that.
  • [Naomi Watts][/p?id=1685] was offered the role of Marlee but turned it due to scheduling conflicts.
  • Other notable actresses auditioning for the role of Marlee included [Bridget Moynahan][/p?id=Bridget%20Moynahan] and [Amanda Peet][/p?id=1026].
  • The much anticipated bathroom scene in [Runaway Jury][/f?id=11882] (2003), where Rohr confronts Finch is the first ever dialog in a movie between [Dustin Hoffman][/p?id=1254] and [Gene Hackman][/p?id=1317]. It was written while the rest of the movie was being filmed, after someone on the crew found out that the two, though they had been friends since 1956, had never starred in a movie together. It was finally shot on a single day at the very end, weeks after both [Hackman][/p?id=1317] and [Hoffman][/p?id=1254] had finished their other work.
