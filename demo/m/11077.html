  • [Macaulay Culkin][/p?id=Macaulay%20Culkin] was considered for the role of Buster.
  • When the Russians roll their car, they get out and one of them says "They broke my watch!" in Russian. This is in reference to a running gag in the first [Blues Brothers][/f?id=11076] movie, where a cop is heard complaining that "They broke my watch!" after every major pile-up, beginning with the cop roll and slide in the mall chase.
  • [James Belushi][/p?id=James%20Belushi] was originally set to co-star with [Aykroyd][/p?id=1196] and Goodman, but couldn't, due to scheduling conflicts.
  • In one scene, Cab ('[Joe Morton'][/p?id=Joe%20Morton]) mentions that when the [Blues Brothers][/f?id=11076] last performed anywhere, they caused, among other things, damages costing $24 million, which was the budget of the original The [Blues Brothers][/f?id=11076] (1980).
  • When the [Blues Brothers][/f?id=11076] are playing "[Ghost Riders][/f?id=14252] In the Sky" at the bluegrass festival, we can hear Elwood say "Duck, give me a mountain tempo in A minor". In the first [Blues Brothers][/f?id=11076] film, when they need to play country music, and decide on "Theme from Rawhide", someone in the band asks "What key?", and Duck Dunn answers: "A. Good country key, A."
  • [Dan Aykroyd][/p?id=1196] reportedly lost 90 lbs to reprise the role of Elwood Blues.
  • Rated #4 in Entertainment Weekly's Top 25 Worst Sequels Ever Made (2008).
