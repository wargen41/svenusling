Visste du?

  • For insurance purposes, and in the event that eighty-year-old Director [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804] was unable to finish shooting the film, [Paul Thomas][/p?id=Paul%20Thomas] Anderson was employed as a standby director.
  • When Guy Noir ([Kevin Kline][/p?id=1547]) popped the cork off of a bottle of champagne, the cork shot off-camera and hit Director [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804] in the forehead. A small cry of "Ow!" can be heard, and [Kline][/p?id=1547] said, "Sorry!" without breaking character.
  • [Maya Rudolph][/p?id=Maya%20Rudolph], who plays the pregnant character Molly, was pregnant during the filming of the movie. The father of [Rudolph's][/p?id=Maya%20Rudolph] unborn child is [Paul Thomas][/p?id=Paul%20Thomas] Anderson, who served as a standby director for [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804] during production of the film.
  • [Meryl Streep][/p?id=1641] accepted her role "in a heartbeat" as it had always been her lifetime's ambition to make a film with [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804].
  • [Robert Altman's][/p?id=1804] last movie.
  • All the music was recorded live.
  • This was the first occasion that [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804] worked with [Meryl Streep][/p?id=1641]. He remarked that she was "twenty-five percent above anyone else".
  • [Garrison Keillor's][/p?id=Garrison%20Keillor] first few drafts of the screenplay were, by his own admission, rather patchy. Director [Robert Altman's][/p?id=1804] only notes on them were "you're getting closer". By the time filming began, [Keillor][/p?id=Garrison%20Keillor] was re-writing scenes constantly.
  • Reportedly, Director [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804] didn't care for the character played by [Virginia Madsen][/p?id=Virginia%20Madsen], and had reduced the role considerably. When [Madsen][/p?id=Virginia%20Madsen] came on-board, she persuaded him to make it a more central character.
  • [Meryl Streep][/p?id=1641] and [Lily Tomlin][/p?id=1585] repeated their on-screen partnership at the 2005 Academy Awards when they presented an Honorary Oscar to [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804].
  • Instead of recording their parts in studio sessions, cast members performed live in the theater, singing and playing their own instruments in front of a real audience of whom [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804] asked to choose their favorite takes.
  • [George Clooney][/p?id=1325] was offered the role of detective Guy Noir, but was forced to turn it down due to scheduling conflicts.
  • [Garrison Keillor][/p?id=Garrison%20Keillor] got the name Lola for [Lindsay Lohan's][/p?id=Lindsay%20Lohan] character from the song "Whatever Lola Wants, Lola Gets" from the musical "Damn Yankees" after [Lohan][/p?id=Lindsay%20Lohan] successfully lobbied for a place in the film, even though there was no part written for her in the original script. Her character's name was also Lola in [Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen][/f?id=Ton%C3%A5rsliv] (2004).
  • [Kevin Kline's][/p?id=1547] character Guy Noir is the main character in one of the serialized stories written by [Garrison Keillor][/p?id=Garrison%20Keillor] for the real "[A Prairie Home Companion][/f?id=10053]" radio show.
  • Prior to being cast, [Virginia Madsen][/p?id=Virginia%20Madsen] was a longtime fan of the radio show, unbeknownst to her agent who mentioned the role to her in passing believing she would not be interested.
  • [Altman][/p?id=1804] reputedly directed most of the film from a wheelchair.
  • The movie cast includes three oscar winners: [Meryl Streep][/p?id=1641], [Tommy Lee Jones][/p?id=1957], and [Kevin Kline][/p?id=1547], and four oscar nominees: [Lily Tomlin][/p?id=1585], [John C. Reilly][/p?id=1474], [Virginia Madsen][/p?id=Virginia%20Madsen], and [Woody Harrelson][/p?id=2011].
