  • Plagued by production problems and script issues, this film's release was delayed for over a year. Over half of the movie had to be re-shot with many cast members being replaced due to scheduling conflicts.
  • [Mandy Moore][/p?id=Mandy%20Moore] was originally cast as Jenny and even filmed her scenes, but was replaced by Mya when the production was halted so the script could be rewritten.
  • When the production was stalled, many cast members were cut, including [Illeana Douglas][/p?id=Illeana%20Douglas], [Heather Langenkamp][/p?id=Heather%20Lang], [Scott Foley][/p?id=Scott%20Foley], [Omar Epps][/p?id=Omar%20Epps], [Robert Forster][/p?id=1812], [James Brolin][/p?id=1411] and [Corey Feldman][/p?id=Corey%20Feldman]. All of these actors even filmed their scenes which were cut when the movie was re-written and re-shot.
  • [Skeet Ulrich][/p?id=Skeet%20Ulrich] originally had a role as the third lead, [Christina Ricci's][/p?id=Christina%20Ricci] love interest, however when the film was re-scripted and preparing to re-shoot, he was unsatisfied with his characters changes and chose to drop out of the film. His character was then written out altogether.
  • The original plot line had three strangers brought together by a car accident in the Hollywood Hills and the subsequent attack of a werewolf. The three characters were named Ellie Hudson ([Christina Ricci][/p?id=Christina%20Ricci]), Vince Winston ([Skeet Ulrich][/p?id=Skeet%20Ulrich]) and Jimmy Myers ([Jesse Eisenberg][/p?id=1453]). When the script was re-written, the plot changed and focused on two estranged siblings who had to fight a curse after a werewolf attack. [Skeet Ulrich's][/p?id=Skeet%20Ulrich] character was totally re-written and renamed Jake. Since Ellie and Jimmy became brother and sister, Ellie's surname was deleted and became Myers.
  • Right before we first see Jimmy and Ellie driving on the road, we can see a mark of the beast made of lights of the town.
