  • Director [Sam Peckinpah][/p?id=Sam%20Peckinpah] insisted on shooting a nude scene for this film featuring one of the female extras. He claimed that this scene would only be included in the European release of the movie. Producer Martin Ransohoff eventually fired [Peckinpah][/p?id=Sam%20Peckinpah] after it was discovered that [Peckinpah][/p?id=Sam%20Peckinpah] intended using the nude scene in the US print as well.
  • [Spencer Tracy][/p?id=1888] was originally cast as Lancey Howard but poor health forced him to withdraw and he was replaced by [Edward G. Robinson][/p?id=Edward%20G.%20Robinson].
  • Mitzi Gaynor campaigned for the role of "Lady Fingers", but it ended up going to [Joan Blondell][/p?id=Joan%20Blondell]. Rumors are abound as to why [Blondell][/p?id=Joan%20Blondell] got the role, with the most common being that Gaynor and Ann-Margret did not quite get along.
  • Final film of [Harry Wilson][/p?id=Harry%20Wilson].
  • [Sharon Tate][/p?id=Sharon%20Tate] was replaced in the film by [Tuesday Weld][/p?id=Tuesday%20Weld] after [Sam Peckinpah][/p?id=Sam%20Peckinpah] was fired by producer Martin Ransohoff.
