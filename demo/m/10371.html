Visste du?

  • Not screened in advance for critics.
  • NASA's liaison for multimedia, Bert Ulrich, has officially stated that "[Apollo 18][/f?id=10371] is not a documentary ... the film is a work of fiction."
  • The prologue text at the beginning of the movie states that the documented footage of the secret [Apollo 18][/f?id=10371] mission was uploaded to the website "www.lunartruth.com". If you type that address into your browser, it redirects you to "www.lunartruth.org" and it displays a page which states that "This website was forcibly censored. It's contents can be seen in the film. DISCOVER THE TRUTH." Also on that page, very well-hidden, is a small white dot that, if clicked on, will send you to the website missioncontrolblog.org - a mock website which was created to "Uncover the truth about the Apollo missions".
  • The opening scenes introducing the three main characters was the last stuff shot for the movie.
  • This film has no score.
  • The script was constantly rewritten during the shooting of the picture.
