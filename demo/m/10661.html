Visste du?

  • In real life, [Mike Tyson][/p?id=Mike%20Tyson] owns seven tigers.
  • No effects or prosthetics were created for Stu's missing tooth. Actor [Ed Helms][/p?id=Ed%20Helms] never had an adult incisor grow, and his fake incisor was taken out for the parts of filming where Stu's tooth is missing.
  • An actual taser was originally going to be used in the taser scene, but lawyers from Warner Bros. demanded that the crew use a prop.
  • [Lindsay Lohan][/p?id=Lindsay%20Lohan] was offered the part of the stripper, Jade, but turned it down. She did not like the script and thought the movie would bomb. [Heather Graham][/p?id=Heather%20Graham] was given the role instead. [Lohan][/p?id=Lindsay%20Lohan] later admitted to regretting the decision.
  • [Jack Black][/p?id=1397] turned down the role of Alan Garner and [Jonah Hill][/p?id=Jonah%20Hill], [Jake Gyllenhaal][/p?id=1407] and [Thomas Haden Church][/p?id=1930] were considered for the role before it was substantially rewritten during development.
  • [Paul Rudd][/p?id=1729] turned down the role of Phil Wenneck.
  • [Mike Tyson][/p?id=Mike%20Tyson] revealed that he appeared in the film to fund his drug habit and that he was high on cocaine when he filmed his scenes. [Tyson][/p?id=Mike%20Tyson] later said that working on the film convinced him to change his lifestyle.
  • [Ed Helms][/p?id=Ed%20Helms], [Zach Galifianakis][/p?id=Zach%20Galifianakis], and [Bradley Cooper][/p?id=1098] were all casual acquaintances before the movie was filmed, which [Helms][/p?id=Ed%20Helms] said he believed helped in establishing a rapport and chemistry amongst their characters. [Helms][/p?id=Ed%20Helms] credited [Todd Phillips][/p?id=Todd%20Phillips] for "bringing together three guys who are really different, but really appreciate each others' humour and sensibilities." [Helms][/p?id=Ed%20Helms] also said the fact that the story of the three characters growing closer and bonding informed the friendship between the three actors: "As you spend 14 hours a day together for three months, you see a lot of sides of somebody. We went through the wringer together, and that shared experience really made us genuine buddies."
  • [Mike Tyson][/p?id=Mike%20Tyson] originally refused to appear in the film, but he changed his mind when he found out that [Todd Phillips][/p?id=Todd%20Phillips] directed [Old School][/f?id=17250] (2003), which [Tyson][/p?id=Mike%20Tyson] liked.
  • [Jeremy Piven][/p?id=Jeremy%20Piven] turned down the part of Stu Price.
  • [Zach Galifianakis][/p?id=Zach%20Galifianakis] improvised the line "I didn't know they gave out rings at the Holocaust". The initial line had Alan making a sexually inappropriate comment about Stu's mom, but Zach thought up the new line for two reasons: he wanted to show that Alan was someone who knew almost nothing about the world, and that a successful joke that mentioned the Holocaust would establish that the film was going to be hilarious overall. Everyone at set loved the joke ([Bradley Cooper][/p?id=1098] appears to be disgusted by it, but he was actually following the script's directions for Phil to look at Alan in contempt, after the original line that was written out) and [Todd Phillips][/p?id=Todd%20Phillips] said it would be in the film.
  • [Malin Akerman][/p?id=Malin%20Akerman] had been considered for the role of Jade that went to [Heather Graham][/p?id=Heather%20Graham].
  • [Todd Phillips][/p?id=Todd%20Phillips] and [Bradley Cooper][/p?id=1098] first film together.
