  • When this film was greeted with derisive laughter by American audiences, Universal decided to market it as a comedy, with the tagline: "Fasten your seatbelts, the thrills are terrific... and so are the laughs!"
  • In its initial screening for industry members and press on the Universal studio lot, this film was laughed off screen.
  • Along with its fellow sequels [AIRPORT 1975][/f?id=15400] and [AIRPORT '77][/f?id=14484], this film was inducted into the RAZZIE Awards Hall of Shame in 1983.
  • [Martha Raye's][/p?id=Martha%20Raye] final feature film.
