  • [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] was the first choice to play Josh Baskin but was unavailable due to scheduling conflicts with the films [Dragnet][/f?id=12958] (1987) and [Punchline][/f?id=17551] (1988). [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] was then offered the lead role, and was rejected because his salary demand ($6 million) was too high. [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] then became available and accepted the lead role for $2 million. [David Moscow][/p?id=David%20Moscow] was originally cast not as young Josh, but as Billy, since he didn't look like [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808]. When [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] was given the role, [David Moscow][/p?id=David%20Moscow] was recast as young Josh.
  • The script was first developed in 1984.
  • To give star [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] an idea of how a 12 year-old would behave, director [Penny Marshall][/p?id=Penny%20Marshall] filmed each "grown-up" scene with [David Moscow][/p?id=David%20Moscow] (Young Josh) playing [Tom Hanks's][/p?id=1950] part, who then copied [David Moscow's][/p?id=David%20Moscow] behavior.
  • In preparation for the role, [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] met [David Moscow][/p?id=David%20Moscow] and studied videotapes of him to see how he behaved and spoke. [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] also felt that [David Moscow][/p?id=David%20Moscow] should just be himself so that the Josh Baskin character persona would be that of a real 12-year-old.
  • [Penny Marshall][/p?id=Penny%20Marshall] became the first female director to ever direct a movie that grossed more than $100 million at the box office with this movie.
  • According to actor [Robert Loggia][/p?id=1814], on the day they filmed the famous keyboard scene at F.A.O. Schwartz, he and [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] noticed that doubles dressed like them were on hand just in case the two could not do the dance moves correctly. It became their goal to do the entire keyboard number without the aid of the doubles. They succeeded.
  • [Albert Brooks][/p?id=Albert%20Brooks] turned down the role of Josh Baskin.
  • [Harrison Ford][/p?id=1358] turned down the role of Josh Baskin.
