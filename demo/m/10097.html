Visste du?

  • [Alejandro Amenábar][/p?id=Alejandro%20Amen%C3%A1bar] wrote the script with [Rachel Weisz][/p?id=1766] in mind to play Hypatia, the lead character.
  • There were days when shooting had to delay for a few seconds. This was because of the nearby churches of Kalkara, Vittoriosa amongst others which have their bells obviously ringing at mid-noon. These bells have been in use for decades. The bells could be heard from the sets of Ricasoli.
  • Originally, [Alejandro Amenábar][/p?id=Alejandro%20Amen%C3%A1bar] wanted [Rachel Weisz][/p?id=1766], [Sacha Baron Cohen][/p?id=Sacha%20Baron%20Cohen] and [Jonathan Rhys Meyers][/p?id=Jonathan%20Rhys%20Meyers] to appear in the film. After reading the screenplay, [Weisz][/p?id=1766] did accept the part of Hypatia, however, [Baron Cohen][/p?id=Sacha%20Baron%20Cohen] turned it down declaring that it was "too prickly, and it would lift sores".
  • The visual effects team designed the night skies accurately for the time period of the movie using a star chart software.
