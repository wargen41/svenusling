Visste du?

  • Madonna and [Sean Penn][/p?id=1868] were approached to star together in this movie, but [Blake Edwards][/p?id=Blake%20Edwards] wanted to cast [Bruce Willis][/p?id=1117] in the male lead and [Melanie Griffith][/p?id=1636] or [Kim Basinger][/p?id=Kim%20Basinger] in the female lead, so Madonna backed out. Eventually [Basinger][/p?id=Kim%20Basinger] would be chosen for the female lead.
  • The script was rewritten so much that writer [Dale Launer][/p?id=Dale%20Launer], who wrote the original script, pretty much disowned the finished film.
  • [Blake Edwards][/p?id=Blake%20Edwards], Leslie Dixon, [Tom Ropelewski][/p?id=Tom%20Ropelewski], and Michael Alan Eddy all did uncredited rewrites on the script.
  • This was [Bruce Willis's][/p?id=1117] first credited film.
  • David Bedford's office window looks out onto the unfinished Fox building, which was soon to star as "Nakatomi Plaza" in [Die Hard][/f?id=12136] (1988). The structure still lacks windows and stone facade on the upper stories and at ground level.
