Visste du?

  • Director [Louis Leterrier][/p?id=Louis%20Leterrier] is a big fan of [Clash of the Titans][/f?id=14368] (1981), having watched it in his childhood.
  • Originally, [Stephen Norrington][/p?id=Stephen%20Norrington] was going to direct the film, but as he hadn't seen the original film he wasn't sure he could make a new yet faithful remake, so in June 2008 he handed the project over to [Louis Leterrier][/p?id=Louis%20Leterrier].
  • Director [Louis Leterrier][/p?id=Louis%20Leterrier] frequently requested that [Ray Harryhausen][/p?id=Ray%20Harryhausen], co-producer and visual effects creator of [Clash of the Titans][/f?id=14368] (1981), be involved in the film. However, [Harryhausen][/p?id=Ray%20Harryhausen] had retired in 1981 and would not be drawn back.
  • [Liam Neeson][/p?id=1584] took a role in this film because his sons are big fans of Greek mythology.
  • [James Woods][/p?id=1426] was hoping to play Hades, a part he had previously done in Hercules (1997) but on a more comedic level.
  • The mechanical owl Bubo from [Clash of the Titans][/f?id=14368] (1981) has a cameo as the toy Perseus picks up before he leaves on his quest. According to the filmmakers, the cameo was widely debated as to whether to keep it in the film or not. It was eventually decided to keep it in the film to please the fans of the original film.
  • When Acrisius is disfigured, he takes the new name of Calibos. Calibos was a character who appeared in [Clash of the Titans][/f?id=14368] (1981).
