Visste du?

  • [Tracey Ullman][/p?id=Tracey%20Ullman] and [Liv Tyler][/p?id=Liv%20Tyler] filmed scenes for this film, but all were cut by director [Woody Allen][/p?id=2010] to reduce running time.
  • [Woody Allen][/p?id=2010] had to tell [Goldie Hawn][/p?id=1340] to try to sing worse because she sang too well to be believable as a normal person just breaking into song.
  • The actors sing their own songs except for [Drew Barrymore][/p?id=1252], who convinced [Woody Allen][/p?id=2010] that her singing was too awful even for the realistic singing voices he was after. She said she doesn't even sing in the shower.
  • [Woody Allen][/p?id=2010] did not tell the actors that it was a musical until after they signed their contracts.
  • The first [Woody Allen][/p?id=2010] film since [Love and Death][/f?id=D%C3%B6d%20Och%20Pina] (1975) to be shot outside of New York.
  • [Drew Barrymore][/p?id=1252] had to wear a wig because her hair was purple at the time.
  • [Drew Barrymore][/p?id=1252] was the only member of the cast to be dubbed for her singing. [Olivia Hayman][/p?id=Olivia%20Hayman] provided her voice.
