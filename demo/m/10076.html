  • The original title was "Whitey & Davey", but it was changed because it sounded "politically incorrect".
  • Davey is based on a picture of [Adam Sandler][/p?id=1003] when he was 19.
  • Originally, Sony Pictures did not want to participate in the animation game with the expense and risks involved, but Columbia chief Amy Pascal greenlit this movie anyway on a personal pitch from [Adam Sandler][/p?id=1003] himself.
  • [Adam Sandler's][/p?id=1003] first full-length animated feature film.
  • Davey's parents are voiced by [Adam Sandler's][/p?id=1003] parents, Stanley and Judy [Sandler][/p?id=1003]. This was Stanley's first and last film appearance, he died in 2003 and his memory was dedicated in another [Adam Sandler][/p?id=1003] film, [50 First Dates][/f?id=13902] (2004).
