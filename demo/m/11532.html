Visste du?

  • While in production on this film in Mexico, [John Wayne][/p?id=1504] won the Golden Globe and found out he was nominated for the Oscar for [True Grit][/f?id=11869] (1969).
  • Although they met some time earlier [Christopher George][/p?id=Christopher%20George] and [Lynda Day George][/p?id=Lynda%20Day%20George] fell in love and later married after working on this project.
  • Both [Ralph Volkie][/p?id=Ralph%20Volkie] and [Trinidad Villa][/p?id=Trinidad%20Villa] are credited as the blacksmith in various reference works.
  • The Zoom in and zoom out sequences of Chisum sitting on his horse on the hillside at the beginning and end of the movie are the same footage. The original shot was the zoom out used at the end. That was reversed to produce the zoom in used at the beginning.
  • When Chisum and Sally are talking on the porch, Chisum opens up a pocket watch to see what time it is and Sally remarks that the picture of a man and a woman inside the watch's cover are her parents (Chisum's brother and sister-in-law). The man in the photograph is actually producer [Andrew J. Fenady][/p?id=Andrew%20J.%20Fenady].
  • The Chisum Ranch house in this film is the exact house used in [Big Jake][/f?id=Big%20Jake] (1971), a [John Wayne][/p?id=1504] western made a year later. Note the scenery around the ranch.
  • [John Wayne][/p?id=1504] was very disappointed that his stunt double was so obvious in the final fight with [Forrest Tucker][/p?id=Forrest%20Tucker].
  • Final film of [Abraham Sofaer][/p?id=Abraham%20Sofaer].
  • [Ray Teal's][/p?id=Ray%20Teal] final movie.
  • [Jim Burk][/p?id=Jim%20Burk] doubled for [Forrest Tucker][/p?id=Forrest%20Tucker] in part of the final fight scene.
  • Film debut of [Geoffrey Deuel][/p?id=Geoffrey%20Deuel].
