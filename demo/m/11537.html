Visste du?

  • [Timothy Dalton][/p?id=1944] pulled out of playing the lead.
  • [Georges Corraface][/p?id=Georges%20Corraface] inherited his leading role from [Timothy Dalton][/p?id=1944] three days before filming began.
  • Even before its release, [Marlon Brando][/p?id=1616] was asking that his name be removed from the credits. According to "Variety", [Brando][/p?id=1616] was upset that the film failed to portray Columbus' complicity in the genocide of Native Americans.
  • The project was concurrently developed with [Ridley Scott's][/p?id=1797] 1492: Conquest of Paradise (1992) to be released on time for the 500th anniversary of the discovery of America.
  • Producers [Alexander][/f?id=10123] and Ilya Salkind's first choice for director was [Ridley Scott][/p?id=1797]. Four months after rejecting their offer, [Scott][/p?id=1797] started working on a rival Christopher Columbus project which ultimately became 1492: Conquest of Paradise (1992). As a consequence of this, the Salkinds unsuccessfully tried to sue [Scott][/p?id=1797] for stealing their idea. They were forced to drop their lawsuit when it was proved that "1492"s producer Alain Goldman and writer Roselyne Bosch's first proposal of a Christopher Columbus project predated theirs.
  • To reach high authenticity the whole crew crossed the Atlantic Ocean from Spain to America in three original ships provided by the Spanish government.
  • It has been alleged that cast and crew did not receive any salaries because of funding problems
