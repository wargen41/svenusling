Visste du?

  • Over 100 actors auditioned for the voice of Reepicheep before [Eddie Izzard][/p?id=1259] was cast. He based his performance on [Errol Flynn][/p?id=Errol%20Flynn].
  • When he was cast, [Ben Barnes][/p?id=Ben%20Barnes] was set to tour as Dakin with the Royal National Theatre's production of "The History Boys". [Barnes][/p?id=Ben%20Barnes] left England without telling the Theatre. They considered suing him for breach of contract but then decided against it. To get into character, [Barnes][/p?id=Ben%20Barnes] wore hair extensions and shaved twice a day. He patterned Caspian's "Spanish" accent on [Mandy Patinkin's][/p?id=Mandy%20Patinkin] Inigo Montoya in [The Princess Bride][/f?id=11042] (1987).
