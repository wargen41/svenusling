  • The Hyde House and Estate in Hyde, Bedfordshire substituted for the Keats House in Hampstead. [Jane Campion][/p?id=Jane%20Campion] decided that the Keats House (also known as Wentworth Place) was too small and "a little bit fusty".
  • [Abbie Cornish][/p?id=Abbie%20Cornish] was the first actress to audition for the part, and waited one month to hear that she got it.
  • [Jane Campion][/p?id=Jane%20Campion] was a member of the jury at the 2007 Venice Film Festival and was especially impressed with [The Assassination of [Jesse James][/p?id=Jesse%20James] by the Coward Robert Ford][/f?id=Mordet%20P%C3%A5%20%5BJesse%20James%5D%5B%2Fp%3Fid%3DJesse%2520James%5D%20Av%20Ynkryggen%20Robert%20Ford], including [Paul Schneider's][/p?id=Paul%20Schneider] performance in the film, so she offered him the role of Charles Armitage Brown in this film.
  • The film shot for one day in Rome. Keats' funeral procession was the last scene to be filmed and the only scene of the film not shot in the UK. This exterior location is the actual residence Keats stayed, and died, in. It now houses the Keats - Shelley House museum.
  • John Keats' poems used in the film are: Endymion, When I Have Fears That I May Cease to Be, The Eve of St Agnes, Ode to a Nightingale, La Belle Dame Sans Merci and [Bright Star][/f?id=11317].
