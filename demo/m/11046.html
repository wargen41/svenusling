Visste du?

  • There is a rumor that the monitor behind Hans Zarkov ([Topol][/p?id=Topol]) as he is having his memory dumped shows scenes from Topol's previous movies.
  • Dino De Laurentiis originally hoped that Federico Fellini would direct this film. The director had actually contributed to the original Flash strip cartoon during WWII.
  • [Kurt Russell][/p?id=1562] auditioned to play Flash Gordon.
  • [Max von Sydow's][/p?id=1632] Ming costume weighed over 70 pounds and he could only stand in it for a few minutes at a time.
  • [Nicolas Roeg][/p?id=Nicolas%20Roeg] was originally going to direct, but didn't due to creative difference. One of his proposals was to excise the trademark cliffhangers and melodrama, seeing Flash as more of "a metaphysical messiah."
  • [Dennis Hopper][/p?id=1238] was considered for the role of Dr Zarkov.
  • [Mike Hodges][/p?id=Mike%20Hodges] was the eighth director chosen.
  • Director [Mike Hodges][/p?id=Mike%20Hodges], referring to the numerous production problems that plagued the film, once called it "the only improvised $27-million movie ever made".
  • [George Lucas][/p?id=George%20Lucas] had hoped to remake the original Flash Gordon (1936/I), but when he learned that Dino De Laurentiis had already bought the rights, he wrote ::Stjärnornas Krig||Star Wars** (1977) instead.
  • First film of [Jim Carter][/p?id=Jim%20Carter].
  • All the main actors were signed for multiple films but the sequels were never made since the first movie didn't do as well as expected.
