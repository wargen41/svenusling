Visste du?

  • Almost every shot in the movie contains at least one white object.
  • Krzysztof Kieslowski was a very precise filmmaker. During the scene in which Dominique has an orgasm, he told [Julie Delpy][/p?id=Julie%20Delpy] exactly how long she had to moan and when she had to start to moan louder.
  • [Julie Delpy][/p?id=Julie%20Delpy] shared that Krzysztof Kieslowski would sit under the camera and chain-smoke. Because he always sat under the camera, he was in very close proximity to the actors during close-ups. She continued that this was reassuring, as he was solely focused on his actors, but sometimes it could be distracting.
