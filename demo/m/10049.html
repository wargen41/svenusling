Visste du?

  • The Folksmen was originally a sketch performed on Saturday Night Live during the 1984-85 season, when [Christopher Guest][/p?id=1166] and [Harry Shearer][/p?id=1363] were cast members and [Michael McKean][/p?id=1659] was the host.
  • In an early-'90s, and again in late 90's/ early 2000's, Spinal Tap tour, [Michael McKean][/p?id=1659], [Harry Shearer][/p?id=1363] and [Christopher Guest][/p?id=1166] opened for themselves as The Folksmen and were booed during the first act, as people did not know or cared that the two bands had the same musicians.
  • All of the songs were written by [Eugene Levy][/p?id=1291], [Christopher Guest][/p?id=1166] and other actors in the cast who also played all their own instruments.
  • In September 2003, much of the cast did a brief tour. They appeared in character, recreating the "reunion concert" as seen at the end of the film.
  • The cover of Mitch and Mickey's record "Meet Mitch and Mickey" is a reference to the cover of "Meet The Beatles".
  • The moaning woman heard through Mitch's ([Eugene Levy's][/p?id=1291]) hotel-room wall was voiced by [Christopher Guest][/p?id=1166].
  • [John Michael Higgins][/p?id=John%20Michael%20Higgins] wrote all of the vocal arrangements for The New Main Street Singers. Originally the group was going to be a nine-piece ensemble that sang in unison (everyone singing the same part) but it was decided to give [Higgins][/p?id=John%20Michael%20Higgins] free reign with it.
  • The Folksmen are a parody of The Kingston Trio, right down to some of the album covers, the uniforms, and the way they perform their songs.
  • According to an interview with [Michael McKean's][/p?id=1659] wife [Annette O'Toole][/p?id=1051], the songs were written by her and [McKean][/p?id=1659] during a car journey to Vancouver from their home in LA. Planes were grounded following the terrorist attacks on the USA on 11 September 2001 and [O'Toole][/p?id=1051] had to get to Vancover to film her TV show Smallville.
  • [Parker Posey][/p?id=Parker%20Posey] learned to play the mandolin for her role as Sissy Knox.
