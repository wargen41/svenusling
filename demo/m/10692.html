  • [Michael Keaton][/p?id=1655] casting as Bruce Wayne/[Batman][/f?id=10692] caused a controversy amongst comic book fans, with 50,000 protest letters sent to Warner Bros. offices. Bob Kane, Sam Hamm and Michael E. Uslan also heavily questioned the casting.
  • [Alec Baldwin][/p?id=1017], [Jeff Bridges][/p?id=1441], [Emilio Estevez][/p?id=1278], [Matthew Broderick][/p?id=1626], [Kevin Costner][/p?id=1544], [Tom Cruise][/p?id=1949], [Michael J. Fox][/p?id=1652], [Harrison Ford][/p?id=1358], [Robert Downey Jr.][/p?id=1809], [Kevin Spacey][/p?id=1550], [Patrick Swayze][/p?id=1718], [Dennis Quaid][/p?id=1239], [Kurt Russell][/p?id=1562], [Arnold Schwarzenegger][/p?id=1062], [Charlie Sheen][/p?id=1149], [Bill Murray][/p?id=1081], [Pierce Brosnan][/p?id=1760], [Tom Selleck][/p?id=1953], [Daniel Day-Lewis][/p?id=1204], [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950] and [Bruce Willis][/p?id=1117] were considered for the role of Bruce Wayne/[Batman][/f?id=10692].
  • [Sean Young][/p?id=1869] was originally cast as Vicki Vale, but broke her collarbone while filming a horse-riding scene with [Michael Keaton][/p?id=1655]. The scene was subsequently written out of the script. [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] suggested replacing [Young][/p?id=1869] with [Michelle Pfeiffer][/p?id=1668] but [Keaton][/p?id=1655], who was in a relationship with [Pfeiffer][/p?id=1668], believed it would be too awkward. She went on to portray [Catwoman][/f?id=11473] in [Batman Returns][/f?id=10693] (1992). [Jon Peters][/p?id=Jon%20Peters] suggested [Kim Basinger][/p?id=Kim%20Basinger] and she was cast.
  • [Robin Williams][/p?id=1828] was considered for the role of The Joker; he would later be considered for The Riddler as well. [Jack Nicholson][/p?id=1402] got the role of The Joker but demanded top-billing and a lucrative deal that gave him royalties on all merchandise.
  • [Willem Dafoe][/p?id=2003], [David Bowie][/p?id=David%20Bowie], [John Lithgow][/p?id=1492], [Tim Curry][/p?id=1938], and [James Woods][/p?id=1426], were considered for The Joker.
  • [Billy Dee Williams][/p?id=Billy%20Dee%20Williams] took the role of [Harvey][/f?id=Harvey] Dent with the expectation that he would be brought back to play Two-Face and reportedly had a contract clause added reserving the role for him. During casting for [Batman Forever][/f?id=10695] (1995) Warner Bros. decided they would prefer [Tommy Lee Jones][/p?id=1957] and bought out [Williams'][/p?id=1828] contract.
  • [Michael Jackson][/p?id=Michael%20Jackson] was asked to write and perform the songs for the movie, but he had to turn it down due to his concert commitments.
  • [Jack Nicholson][/p?id=1402] received a percentage of the gross on the film, and due to its massive box-office took home around $60 million. As of 2003 it is still the single-movie record for actor's salary.
  • In the original script with Robin included, the Flying Graysons (John, Mary, and Dick) are introduced at the parade scene. The Joker shoots the trapeze artists sending John and Mary to their deaths and leaving Dick to survive. Dick later becomes Robin in full costume at the end. The special edition version of the DVD release of [Batman][/f?id=10692] (1989) features an animated storyboard sequence of this scene, where Dick Grayson was voiced by Jason Hillhouse, and [Batman][/f?id=10692] and the Joker were voiced by Kevin Conroy and [Mark Hamill][/p?id=1609] respectively.
  • [Jack Nicholson][/p?id=1402] said of his role, "The thing I like about The Joker is that his sense of humor is completely tasteless."
  • The only two actors to appear in all four [Burton][/p?id=1937]/Schumacher films are [Pat Hingle][/p?id=Pat%20Hingle] (Commissioner Gordon) and [Michael Gough][/p?id=Michael%20Gough] (Alfred.)
