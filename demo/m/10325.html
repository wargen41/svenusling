  • When [Meg Ryan][/p?id=1633] was offered the role of Anya, she could not decide if she wanted to accept it or not. Upon hearing of [Ryan's][/p?id=1633] indecision, Fox took an audio clip of [Ryan][/p?id=1633] talking in [Sleepless in Seattle][/f?id=20033] (1993) and created a short animated sequence of Anya speaking the lines. They sent the clip to [Ryan][/p?id=1633], and she was so impressed that she changed her mind and accepted the role.
