  • [Colin Salmon][/p?id=Colin%20Salmon] was considered for the role of Machine Gun Joe Viterbo.
  • [David Carradine][/p?id=1213], who starred in the original [Death Race 2000][/f?id=11897] (1975) plays the voice of Frankenstein in the opening scene.
  • According to writer and director [Paul W.S. Anderson][/p?id=Paul%20W.S.%20Anderson], it's a prequel to [Death Race 2000][/f?id=11897] (1975) set 15 to 20 years before even though it's actually set sometime after 2012.
  • Originally written as a sequel to [Death Race 2000][/f?id=11897] (1975) titled Death Race 3000, a race around the world with futuristic hovering, invisible and transformer cars, but had to be rewritten due to production costs.
  • [Tom Cruise][/p?id=1949] and Paula Wagner were originally attached as producers. [Roger Corman][/p?id=Roger%20Corman], producer of the original [Death Race 2000][/f?id=11897] (1975), had an option agreement with [Cruise][/p?id=1949], with the idea that [Cruise][/p?id=1949] would play the lead. This never got off the ground as [Cruise][/p?id=1949] was unhappy with the first 3 versions of the script that had been submitted.
  • [Roger Corman's][/p?id=Roger%20Corman] biggest budgeted film in years.
  • This remake largely came about after [Roger Corman][/p?id=Roger%20Corman] saw and liked [Paul W.S. Anderson's][/p?id=Paul%20W.S.%20Anderson] first film Shopping (1994). [Corman][/p?id=Roger%20Corman] secured the rights to show the film in the US and at that point asked Anderson what film he'd like to make next. Anderson cited this one, though it ultimately took 13 years for that to come to fruition.
  • [Joan Allen][/p?id=Joan%20Allen] was the production's first choice to play the female warden. Much to [Paul W.S. Anderson's][/p?id=Paul%20W.S.%20Anderson] surprise, [Allen][/p?id=Joan%20Allen] was just as keen to do the film, wanting to shake up her image.
  • One of the remits for [Jason Statham][/p?id=1438] and his numerous fight scenes in the film was that he should never resort to martial arts as that would be too reminiscent of his [Transporter][/f?id=20730] films.
