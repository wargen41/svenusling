Visste du?

  • Dean Richmond was named after Bill Richmond, co-writer of the original [The Nutty Professor][/f?id=Dr.%20J%C3%A4kel%20Och%20Mr.%20Hyde].
  • The family scene was nearly cut out of the final film due to lack of relevance with the film's plot.
  • The abusive nightclub comic is named Reggie Warrington after [Reginald Hudlin][/p?id=Reginald%20Hudlin] and [Warrington Hudlin][/p?id=Warrington%20Hudlin], who worked with [Eddie Murphy][/p?id=1261] on the film [Boomerang][/f?id=11265].
  • In the scene with [Eddie Murphy][/p?id=1261] and [Dave Chappelle][/p?id=Dave%20Chappelle], their dialogue was mostly improvised.
