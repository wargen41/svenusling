  • This [Hal Roach][/p?id=Hal%20Roach] comedy starred [Stan Laurel][/p?id=1890] and [Oliver Hardy][/p?id=1702] and had a costumed mouse which looked strangely like Mickey Mouse, a Walt Disney character. The 1961 Disney remake (Babes in Toyland (1961)) featured two comedians obviously impersonating [Laurel][/p?id=1890] and [Hardy][/p?id=1702].
  • During one of the Three Little Pig segments , an instrumental version of "Who's Afraid of The Big Bad Wolf?" is heard on the soundtrack.
  • [Hal Roach][/p?id=Hal%20Roach] and Walt Disney were good friends, and it paid off for [Roach][/p?id=Hal%20Roach] in this picture. [Roach][/p?id=Hal%20Roach] asked to borrow the tune "Who's Afraid of the Big Bad Wolf?" from a Disney cartoon of the previous year, and Disney obliged.
