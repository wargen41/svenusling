Visste du?

  • [Dwight Henry][/p?id=Dwight%20Henry] owned and operated the bakery across the street from the space where the crew was working and casting. [Benh Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] posted a casting flyer with tearaway numbers in Dwight's bakery. After several weeks, he invited Dwight over for a read. Two days later, Dwight moved his shop to a larger space, and when the crew went looking for him for a call back, no one knew where he went. When they located Dwight 2 months later, he turned them down because he was investing all his time in the new bakery. Finally, every single person involved with the film at that point showed up at his bakery at the same time and told him he had to do the movie. Dwight agreed, as long as they rehearsed with him during his midnight baker's hours.
  • On the film's very first day of shooting in the fictional "Bathtub" location outside of New Orleans, the BP oil rig explosion and the start of the massive spill occurred. For most of the shoot in nearby waters, [Benh Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] and his crew had to maneuver in and around the clean-up operations.
  • Wink reaches down in the river to show Hushpuppy how to catch a fish with his bare hand. A member of the production crew almost died swimming under water while shooting the scene.
  • [Benh Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] gives considerable credit to Qulyndreia [Wallis][/p?id=Quvenzhan%C3%A9%20Wallis], mother of the movie's nine-year-old star [Quvenzhané Wallis][/p?id=Quvenzhan%C3%A9%20Wallis] who was always on the set and helped [Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] explain concepts and draw emotions from her daughter to maximize the quality of her Oscar-nominated performance.
  • [Quvenzhané Wallis][/p?id=Quvenzhan%C3%A9%20Wallis] and her mom admit that they fibbed about Quvenzhané's age, claiming she was at least six years old, as required to audition, when she was only five. According to director [Benh Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin], Nazie, as she is called, beat out almost 4,000 other area kids considered for the lead role.
  • More than half of the cast hails from Louisiana bayou country, including the film's star [Quvenzhané Wallis][/p?id=Quvenzhan%C3%A9%20Wallis], and five others in speaking roles.
  • With the announcement of the 2012 Academy Award (Oscar) nominations on January 10, 2013, [Quvenzhané Wallis][/p?id=Quvenzhan%C3%A9%20Wallis] became the youngest person ever nominated for a Best Actress Oscar (at age 9). She broke the record of the previous youngest-ever Best Actress nominee, [Keisha Castle-Hughes][/p?id=Keisha%20Castle-Hughes], who was nominated for [Whale Rider][/f?id=21916] at age 13. The same day that [Wallis][/p?id=Quvenzhan%C3%A9%20Wallis] became the youngest-ever Best Actress nominee, [Emmanuelle Riva][/p?id=Emmanuelle%20Riva] (age 85) became the oldest-ever Best Actress nominee for her role in [Amour][/f?id=Amour] (2012). Oscar host [Seth MacFarlane][/p?id=Seth%20MacFarlane] made the joke at the ceremony "To the nominees, congratulations, you were nominated for an Oscar. Something a 9 year old could do."
  • The movie was financed, with a reported budget of $1.3 million, by New York-based nonprofit Cinereach and was the first feature-length project of Court 13 Pictures.
  • The production did not hire a cinematographer until less than a month before production. Director [Benh Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] had originally intended to act as cinematographer himself but later decided to employ one. Ben Richardson was hired as as the cinematographer for the miniature portions and had lobbied to shoot the entire film, but despite his friendship with [Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] the producers were hesitant to hire Richardson because he had never shot a feature. After veteran cinematographers were screened and an appropriate one could not be found Richardson shot a test reel on his own and presented it to the producers, and was subsequently hired.
  • Director [Benh Zeitlin][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] revealed in a June 22, 2012, interview with HitFix that he never looked at dailies until after the shoot was finished.
  • The movie was sold to Fox Searchlight Pictures for about $2 million.
  • It is the only Best Picture Oscar nominee that year that did not win any Academy Awards.
  • [Benh Zeitlin's][/p?id=Benh%20Zeitlin] directorial film debut.
