Visste du?

  • The role of Paul Kersey was originally intended for [Steve McQueen][/p?id=1904], who turned it down.
  • [Frank Sinatra][/p?id=1302] was offered the role of Paul Kersey, but backed out.
  • Dino De Laurentiis and Paramount originally wanted to call the film "The Sidewalk Vigilante" because they thought a movie with "Death" in the title was a deterrent and would put audiences off.
  • According to writer Brian Garfield, [Sidney Lumet][/p?id=1879] was set to direct the film with [Jack Lemmon][/p?id=1400] playing [Paul Kersey][/p?id=Paul%20Kersey] (presumably to be more in line with the "everyman" character in the book) and [Henry Fonda][/p?id=1371] as the police chief. After [Lumet][/p?id=1879] chose to direct [Serpico][/f?id=18244] (1973) instead, both [Lemmon][/p?id=1400] and [Fonda][/p?id=1371] dropped out. At one point the movie was also set to be shot in black-and-white.
  • Film debut of [Jeff Goldblum][/p?id=1443].
  • After the success of [Dirty Harry][/f?id=12158] (1971), [Clint Eastwood][/p?id=1184] was offered the role of Paul Kersey but declined, feeling he would be poorly cast. He also thought that [Gregory Peck][/p?id=1345] would have been right for the part.
  • [Charles Bronson][/p?id=1144] was about fifty-two years of age when he appeared in this movie.
  • Playing a young police patrol officer, Patrolman Jackson Reilly, this movie features actor [Christopher Guest][/p?id=1166] in one of his earliest screen roles, his third.
