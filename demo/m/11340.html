Visste du?

  • [Nicole Kidman][/p?id=1697] and [Anthony Hopkins][/p?id=1053] were cast originally, but both had to bow out due to scheduling conflicts.
  • Both [Terry Gilliam][/p?id=1926] and [Matt Damon][/p?id=1624] wanted to hire [Samantha Morton][/p?id=Samantha%20Morton] for the female lead, but Bob Weinstein and Harvey Weinstein vetoed this.
  • Goran Bregovic was [Terry Gilliam's][/p?id=1926] original choice for composer. When [Gilliam][/p?id=1926] decided to go with a more traditional score, Bregovic was replaced by Dario Marianelli.
  • [Robin Williams][/p?id=1828] was originally cast in the film, but pulled out of the project.
  • [Johnny Depp][/p?id=1505] was originally set to star as Will Grimm.
  • Cinematographer [Nicola Pecorini][/p?id=Nicola%20Pecorini] was personally fired by Bob Weinstein.
  • According to [Terry Gilliam][/p?id=1926], [Matt Damon][/p?id=1624] took tango lessons in preparation for this film.
  • In June 2004, a stalemate occurred between Bob Weinstein and [Terry Gilliam][/p?id=1926] over the final cut of the film. [Terry Gilliam][/p?id=1926] decided to place the film on hold for six months. He went on to do [Tideland][/f?id=Tideland] (2005) and came back to finish this feature in January 2005. In his version.
  • The "hand forest" sequence in this film is an idea [Terry Gilliam][/p?id=1926] has had for almost 25 years. It was originally written for [Time Bandits][/f?id=20384] (1981) and almost used in [Brazil][/f?id=11299] (1985).
  • [Matt Damon][/p?id=1624] and [Heath Ledger][/p?id=1366] were originally cast in opposite roles. They petitioned and switched their roles.
  • According to the director, [Matt Damon's][/p?id=1624] hair style was based on the British TV chef Jamie Oliver.
