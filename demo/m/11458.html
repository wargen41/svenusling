  • The first official Bond film to start without the famous gun barrel intro. It does not return until the end of the following film, [Quantum Of Solace][/f?id=17575] (2008).
  • James Bond's letter of resignation via the Mi6 intelligence intranet read: "M - I hereby tender my resignation with immediate effect. Sincerely, James Bond". This is the third James Bond movie where James Bond has resigned. The first was [On Her Majesty's Secret Service][/f?id=14989] (1969) and the second was [Licence to Kill][/f?id=20370] (1989). In the latter, his license to kill was revoked and in [Die Another Day][/f?id=12135] (2002) he was temporarily decommissioned.
  • [Daniel Craig][/p?id=1203] is the first actor to play James Bond who is younger than the series itself.
  • [Daniel Craig's][/p?id=1203] role in the British thriller [Layer Cake][/f?id=15672] (2004) is said to have clinched the role of James Bond for him over the other competing rival actors. DVD / Video Rentals of the movie went up after the announcement [Daniel Craig][/p?id=1203] would be the new James Bond. The [Layer Cake][/f?id=15672] (2004) end-of-movie Bondesque line has [Craig][/p?id=1203] say "My name? If you knew that, you'd be as clever as me. " evoking the famous catchphrase "The name is Bond, James Bond".
  • This is the first film since [Dr. No][/f?id=10095] (1962) where there were no nude dancing females during the opening title sequence.
  • During 2004, [Quentin Tarantino][/p?id=1763] had suggested making a version of "Casino Royale" with [Pierce Brosnan][/p?id=1760]. Reportedly, [Tarantino][/p?id=1763] and [Brosnan][/p?id=1760] spoke publicly about this proposed project. When [Brosnan][/p?id=1760] was not rehired as Bond, [Tarantino][/p?id=1763] very publicly berated the producers and refused to have anything more to do with the project even though he was never attached to the picture at any time.
  • [Daniel Craig][/p?id=1203] quit smoking and had Simon Waterson as a personal trainer to get into shape.
  • [Pierce Brosnan][/p?id=1760] had expressed an interest in making this film, but was considered too old at 53.
