Everything that had been shot was re-shot except for long shots which to this day remain as [Roy Thinnes][/p?id=Roy%20Thinnes] and not [William Devane][/p?id=William%20Devane].
  • [Alfred Hitchcock][/p?id=1021] was famous for making his actors follow the script to the word, but in this movie he let the characters improvise and use their own dialogue.
