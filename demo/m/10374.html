  • The name of [Tom Felton's][/p?id=Tom%20Felton] character Dodge Landon is a reference to the characters Dodge ([Jeff Burton][/p?id=Jeff%20Burton]) and Landon ([Robert Gunner][/p?id=Robert%20Gunner]), Colonel Taylor's ([Charlton Heston][/p?id=1151]) fellow astronauts in [Planet of the Apes][/f?id=10372].
  • The orangutan character befriended by Caeser in prison is named Maurice after actor [Maurice Evans][/p?id=Maurice%20Evans], who played the orangutan Doctor Zaius in the original [Planet of the Apes][/f?id=10372].
  • This is the first "Planet of the Apes" film in which neither [Charlton Heston][/p?id=1151] nor [Roddy McDowall][/p?id=Roddy%20McDowall], the stars of the original [Planet of the Apes][/f?id=10372], are involved in the actual film making. [Heston][/p?id=1151] died on April 5, 2008 at the age of 84 whereas [McDowall][/p?id=Roddy%20McDowall] died on October 3, 1998 at the age of 70.
  • [Jamie Harris][/p?id=Jamie%20Harris] plays Rodney, the sympathetic keeper in the ape sanctuary. This is a nod to legendary ape actor [Roddy McDowall][/p?id=Roddy%20McDowall].
  • [Charlton Heston][/p?id=1151] makes two appearances - he is featured on the TV screen in the role of Michelangelo from [The Agony and the Ecstasy][/f?id=21524] in the ape sanctuary bunk house, and in a similar scene, with [Tom Felton's][/p?id=Tom%20Felton] character watching [Heston][/p?id=1151] play the role of Moses in [The Ten Commandments][/f?id=11878].
  • In one scene, Caesar is shown handling a 3D puzzle of the Statue of Liberty, in reference to the the ending of the original [Planet of the Apes][/f?id=10372].
  • [Kathryn Bigelow][/p?id=1534], [Robert Rodriguez][/p?id=Robert%20Rodriguez], [Tomas Alfredson][/p?id=Tomas%20Alfredson], Allen Hughes and Albert Hughes, [Pierre Morel][/p?id=Pierre%20Morel], [James McTeigue][/p?id=James%20McTeigue], Dennis Iliadis and Scott Charles Stewart all were considered by the studio, or passed on the project before director [Rupert Wyatt][/p?id=Rupert%20Wyatt] signed on.
  • There is a reference regarding the first space mission to Mars in the film saying that the spacecraft/astronauts have disappeared. A subtle way of the filmmakers to indicate that they gone into a time warp and will end up on the future "[Planet of the Apes][/f?id=10372]".
  • The lab worker who cares for the chimps is named Franklin, a nod to the director of the original [Planet of the Apes][/f?id=10372] film, [Franklin J. Schaffner][/p?id=Franklin%20J.%20Schaffner].
  • This movie is the first installment to feature another ape species besides chimpanzees, gorillas, and orangutans; Koba, the scarred lab ape, is a bonobo.
