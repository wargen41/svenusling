Visste du?

  • The word "Fuck" is said 422 times, including in the narration - 2.4 times per minute on average. The film also holds the Guinness world record for the most swearing in a film.
  • [Sharon Stone][/p?id=1874] spent many long workdays in agony while filming scenes for this film. She has back trouble due to an old injury, and the gold & white beaded gown she wears during a casino scene weighed 45 pounds.
  • [Jamie Lee Curtis][/p?id=1428], Madonna, [Nicole Kidman][/p?id=1697], and [Michelle Pfeiffer][/p?id=1668] were considered for the role of Ginger. [Traci Lords][/p?id=Traci%20Lords] was seriously considered after an excellent audition. [Sean Young][/p?id=1869] turned down the role due to pregnancy. [Melanie Griffith][/p?id=1636] was almost cast, but [Sharon Stone][/p?id=1874] convinced director [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] to give her the role.
  • According to [Alan King][/p?id=Alan%20King], 'Frank "Lefty' Rosenthal', upon whom the [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] character was based, wanted [Richard Widmark][/p?id=1793] to play the lead in the film. However, [Widmark][/p?id=1793] was 80 years old by that time, and, therefore, not a practical choice.
  • To avoid the continuity problems that accompany a chain-smoking movie character, [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] always held his cigarettes the same distance from the lit end so that their lengths never appear to change.
  • When [James Woods][/p?id=1426] heard that [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] was interested in working with him, [Woods][/p?id=1426] called [Scorsese's][/p?id=1619] office and left the following message: "Any time, any place, any part, any fee."
  • To date (summer 2009) this is the last theatrical movie to be censored by Swedish authorities. The scene cut is the infamous "head in the vise" scene.
