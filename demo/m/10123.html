Visste du?

  • [Colin Farrell][/p?id=Colin%20Farrell] broke his ankle and arm during filming when he fell down a stairway.
  • [Val Kilmer][/p?id=1973] gained over 50 pounds and stopped working out for the plump role of Philip.
  • The biography of [Alexander][/f?id=10123] by Oxford University professor Robin Lane Fox was an original inspiration and source of information for director [Oliver Stone][/p?id=1705]. As an historical advisor, Professor Fox didn't get an on screen credit; his price for giving his advice was to be allowed to take a place at the head of what is one of the largest cavalry charges ever filmed. Professor Fox was used to riding around the English countryside, but gladly dressed up as a Macedonean cavalry officer to live his dream of charging for [Alexander][/f?id=10123].
  • [Brad Pitt][/p?id=1096] was considered for the role of Hephaestion, but turned down the role due to personal concerns with the character.
  • [Oliver Stone][/p?id=1705] previously tried to mount the project with [Tom Cruise][/p?id=1949] in the title role.
  • [Sean Connery][/p?id=1866] was originally considered for the role that was eventually offered to [Val Kilmer][/p?id=1973].
  • [Oliver Stone][/p?id=1705] and [Val Kilmer][/p?id=1973] originally discussed [Alexander][/f?id=10123] while filming The Doors (1991).
  • [Val Kilmer][/p?id=1973] had to undergo over an hour of daily makeup to look older and don the scar tissue that covers the eye that Philip lost in battle.
  • [Heath Ledger][/p?id=1366] was originally considered for the role of [Alexander][/f?id=10123] before [Colin Farrell][/p?id=Colin%20Farrell] was cast.
  • [Angelina Jolie][/p?id=1043] plays [Colin Farrell's][/p?id=Colin%20Farrell] mother even though she is less than a year older than he is.
  • [Oliver Stone's][/p?id=1705] first choice for the role of King Philip was [Liam Neeson][/p?id=1584], who turned it down.
  • Director [Oliver Stone's][/p?id=1705] first choice for the role of [Alexander][/f?id=10123] the Great was [Russell Crowe][/p?id=1846], who turned it down.
  • The Irish accents you hear from the Macedonian soldiers were not there to accommodate [Colin Farrell][/p?id=Colin%20Farrell] as some have asserted. As Macedonians were mostly from more remote hill tribes and were coarser and less civilized than the Greeks, the Celtic accents used in the movie serve to portray fictionally the difference between them and the more refined Greeks, who speak in a more upper class, English accent. Many critics and audience members failed to understand this device.
