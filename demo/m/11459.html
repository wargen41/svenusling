Visste du?

  • A scene was filmed with [Zelda Rubinstein][/p?id=Zelda%20Rubinstein] reprising her role from [Poltergeist][/f?id=Poltergeist] (1982) (shooting out of a chimney and shouting "Go toward the light!") but was not included in the final cut.
  • This was the first film with a computer-animated title character.
  • In the breakfast scene, [Casper's][/f?id=11459] uncles enter the kitchen twirling like helicopters while Wagner's "Ride of the Valkyries" plays in the background, and Stretch later says "I love the smell of fleshies in the morning!" All this is a direct reference to [Apocalypse Now][/f?id=10366] (1979).
  • A live-action sequel was proposed at the time that principal filming wrapped on '[Casper'][/f?id=11459] and a treatment was written but a combination of less-than-stellar box office gross (although the film grossed $100 million in the US, the budget was more than half of that total and analysts predicted much higher earnings for the film) and the fact that both [Christina Ricci][/p?id=Christina%20Ricci] and [Bill Pullman][/p?id=1084] were attached to other projects after [Casper][/f?id=11459] and would not be available until more than a year later caused the idea to be scrapped. The producers then decided to scrap the live action sequel ideas and instead produce a cartoon series to continue the story.
