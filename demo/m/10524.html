Visste du?

  • [Frank Perry][/p?id=Frank%20Perry] did not finish directing this film because of creative differences.
  • The scene between [Burt Lancaster][/p?id=1120] and [Janice Rule][/p?id=Janice%20Rule] was directed by [Sydney Pollack][/p?id=1914].
  • According to multiple biographies of [Burt Lancaster][/p?id=1120], Barbara Loden's scene with [Lancaster][/p?id=1120] was reshot with [Janice Rule][/p?id=Janice%20Rule] as Loden's performance had overpowered him in the scene. Using [Rule][/p?id=Janice%20Rule], whose performance was less powerful than that of Loden, restored balance to the scene.
  • Cameo (John Cheever): (author of the short story "The Swimmer") at the pool party.
  • Filmed in the summer of 1966 but not released until 1968.
  • Siouxsie and the Banshees' 1978 debut album "The [Scream][/f?id=18022]" was inspired by this film.
  • [Burt Lancaster][/p?id=1120] did numerous push-ups and sit-ups every morning before each shoot to stay lean and solid. He gained 20 lb. of muscle by doing weights, running, karate and aerobics.
  • The film was an allegory for the failure of the [American Dream][/f?id=10297].
  • Although the critical response at the time was mixed, critics were unanimous in their praise of [Burt Lancaster's][/p?id=1120] physique, especially considering he was 52 at the time of filming.
