Visste du?

  • Maria Shriver said in a TV interview that at the premiere of this film she remarked to her then-husband [Arnold Schwarzenegger][/p?id=1062], "If this doesn't kill your career, nothing will".
  • [Arnold Schwarzenegger's][/p?id=1062] character was originally intended to be a reprisal of Conan, star of the comic book in which [Red Sonja][/f?id=Red%20Sonja] first appeared, but the film did not have the rights to this name. An unofficial explanation endorsed by fans is that Kalidor is one of Conan's "traveling names," a common feature of multi-national mythical/legendary heroes such as Zeus aka Jupiter and J.R.R. Tolkien's Gandalf aka Mithrandir.
  • [Arnold Schwarzenegger][/p?id=1062] himself eschews this movie as one of his worst, yet he (laughingly) claims that it's an excellent disciplinary tool for his children: "I tell them, if they get on my bad side, they'll be forced to watch [][/f?id=10676]Red Sonja:::: (1985) ten times in a row. Consequently, none of my kids has ever given me much trouble."
  • [Arnold Schwarzenegger][/p?id=1062] and [Brigitte Nielsen][/p?id=Brigitte%20Nielsen] had an affair during filming.
  • [Arnold Schwarzenegger][/p?id=1062] signed up for a glorified cameo, as a favor to producer Dino De Laurentiis. Much to his surprise, he was stuck on the set for four weeks, three weeks longer than expected. He discovered after watching a rough cut of the movie that his role had been expanded to co-star, thanks to crafty angles and multiple cameras. Soon after, [Schwarzenegger][/p?id=1062] decided to terminate his 10-year contract with De Laurentiss.
  • [Sigourney Weaver][/p?id=1882] was considered for [Red Sonja][/f?id=Red%20Sonja].
  • In 2009, a remake of the film was announced, with [Robert Rodriguez][/p?id=Robert%20Rodriguez] to direct and [Rose McGowan][/p?id=Rose%20McGowan] to star as [Red Sonja][/f?id=Red%20Sonja], succeeding [Brigitte Nielsen][/p?id=Brigitte%20Nielsen] in the role. However, due to the failure of the 2011 remake of [Conan The Barbarian][/f?id=11654] (2011), production was abandoned and both [Rodriguez][/p?id=Robert%20Rodriguez] and [McGowan][/p?id=Rose%20McGowan] backed out of the project. As of 2015, the reboot is in development hell.
  • [Sandahl Bergman][/p?id=Sandahl%20Bergman] was initially cast as the title character, but decided on portraying "Queen Gedren" instead, to help avoid typecasting.
  • Critic Gene Siskel called the film "A bad film that was unintentionally funny".
