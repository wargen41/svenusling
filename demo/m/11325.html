Visste du?

  • [John Travolta][/p?id=1501] was given the choice of playing either Deakins or Hale in the film. He chose the villain Deakins.
  • Director [John Woo][/p?id=John%20Woo] originally wanted Hale to die during the film.
  • Oliver [Wood][/p?id=John%20Woo] was considered to be the film's director of photography, but he was unable to take the job, due to the broken ankle he sustained while shooting [Cutthroat Island][/f?id=11738] (1995). He was replaced by Peter Levy (who had also replaced him on [Cutthroat Island][/f?id=11738]). Director [John Woo][/p?id=John%20Woo] later picked [Wood][/p?id=John%20Woo] to shoot his next film, [Face/Off][/f?id=13536] (1997).
  • In an interview on "Parkinson" (1971), [Halle Berry][/p?id=Halle%20Berry] recalled that the producers turned her down for the role of Terry Carmichael and told her "there are no black park rangers".
  • [Jennifer Aniston][/p?id=1446] was considered for the role of park ranger Terry Carmichael.
  • [Christian Slater][/p?id=1164] turned down a role in Assassins (1995) to work with director [John Woo][/p?id=John%20Woo].
  • As preparation for his role in this movie, [Christian Slater][/p?id=1164] gave up smoking.
  • [John Travolta][/p?id=1501] pocketed $7 million for appearing in the film.
