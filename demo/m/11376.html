Visste du?

  • After working on this film, [Warren Beatty][/p?id=1991] and [Annette Bening][/p?id=1050] were married. They'd met several years before when she auditioned for another [Beatty][/p?id=1991] production. They met again when she was seriously considered for the role of Tess Trueheart in [Dick Tracy][/f?id=12134].
  • The movie that [George Raft][/p?id=George%20Raft] ([Joe Mantegna][/p?id=Joe%20Mantegna]) is making while Siegel visits him on the set is [Manpower][/f?id=En%20Kvinna%20Bland%20M%C3%A4n] which co-starred [Edward G. Robinson][/p?id=Edward%20G.%20Robinson]. The scene being filmed where [Raft][/p?id=George%20Raft] gets into a bar fight and hands a broken chair to an actress (Virginia Hill ([Annette Bening][/p?id=1050] in Bugsy) really does appear in the actual film.
  • The film shows Siegel watching a screen test of himself. In real life, Bugsy Siegel made many friends amongst the Hollywood elite, asked for and had a screen test. The footage no longer exists, like so many other screen tests, yet the legend of Siegel's attempt to break into showbiz lives on.
