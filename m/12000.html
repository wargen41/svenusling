Visste du?

  • [Lana Turner][/p?id=Lana%20Turner] took a much smaller salary, than her usual $25,000 per week and worked for 50% of the film's profits, which earned her over $2 million (setting a record for an actress at the time).
  • [Natalie Wood][/p?id=Natalie%20Wood] was considered for the part played by [Susan Kohner][/p?id=Susan%20Kohner].
  • [Douglas Sirk's][/p?id=Douglas%20Sirk] last Hollywood film before he retired back to his native Germany.
  • '[Margaret O'Brien'][/p?id=Margaret%20O'Brien] originally considered for role of Sara Jane, that ultimately went to [Susan Kohner][/p?id=Susan%20Kohner].
  • [Pearl Bailey][/p?id=Pearl%20Bailey] was originally considered for the role of Annie Johnson.
  • [Juanita Moore][/p?id=Juanita%20Moore] appeared as Annie Johnson in this [Douglas Sirk][/p?id=Douglas%20Sirk] film. Many years later, the [Sirkian][/p?id=Douglas%20Sirk] Technicolor 1950s melodrama homage [Far From Heaven][/f?id=13733] would star the similarly named [Julianne Moore][/p?id=1524].
  • In Sarah Jane's room while she is dancing by herself, you will see a picture of [Bobby Darin][/p?id=Bobby%20Darin].
