Visste du?

  • The device, on the heel of his boot, that the [Batman][/f?id=10692] uses to summon a swarm of bats is taken directly from [Frank Miller's][/p?id=Frank%20Miller] "Batman: Year One".
  • A second stage Scarecrow look was created for the hallucination scenes but was never shown on film. The mask was more organic and tighter around the actor's face.
  • Before [Christian Bale][/p?id=1163] was cast as [Batman][/f?id=10692], many other actors were considered for the role, including [Ashton Kutcher][/p?id=Ashton%20Kutcher], [David Boreanaz][/p?id=David%20Boreanaz] and newcomer [Hugh Dancy][/p?id=Hugh%20Dancy].
  • Only days before the role of [Batman][/f?id=10692] was cast, eight actors were asked to audition for the part. The actors were [Christian Bale][/p?id=1163], [Joshua Jackson][/p?id=Joshua%20Jackson], [Eion Bailey][/p?id=Eion%20Bailey], [Hugh Dancy][/p?id=Hugh%20Dancy], [Billy Crudup][/p?id=Billy%20Crudup], [Cillian Murphy][/p?id=Cillian%20Murphy], [Henry Cavill][/p?id=Henry%20Cavill] and [Jake Gyllenhaal][/p?id=1407]. While [Bale][/p?id=1163] won the part, [Christopher Nolan][/p?id=1170] liked [Murphy's][/p?id=Cillian%20Murphy] audition so much, he cast him as Dr. [Jonathan Crane][/p?id=Jonathan%20Crane]/The Scarecrow.
  • [Christian Bale][/p?id=1163] lost his voice three times during filming after altering his voice while playing Batman.
  • During filming of the scene where [Batman][/f?id=10692] is being towed by a train through the streets of Gotham, so much steam was used that it would "rain" on the cast and crew for several minutes after each take.
  • Director [Christopher Nolan][/p?id=1170] decided that there would be no second unit, and so for the whole of the one hundred and twenty nine shooting days [Nolan][/p?id=1170] oversaw every shot of the film personally.
  • [Christopher Eccleston][/p?id=Christopher%20Eccleston] was rumored for the role of Dr. [Jonathan Crane][/p?id=Jonathan%20Crane]/The Scarecrow before [Cillian Murphy][/p?id=Cillian%20Murphy] was eventually cast.
  • [Christian Bale][/p?id=1163] had previously screen tested and was considered for the role of Robin in [Batman Forever][/f?id=10695] (1995).
  • [Chris Cooper][/p?id=1157] turned down the role of James Gordon.
  • [Kurt Russell][/p?id=1562] and [Dennis Quaid][/p?id=1239] were considered for the role of James Gordon.
  • [Viggo Mortensen][/p?id=1977] turned down [Liam Neeson's][/p?id=1584] role. [Daniel Day-Lewis][/p?id=1204] was also approached.
  • [Anthony Hopkins][/p?id=1053] was offered the role of Alfred but declined.
  • [Laurence Fishburne][/p?id=1567] was considered for the role of Lucius Fox.
  • [David S. Goyer][/p?id=David%20S.%20Goyer] mentioned in an interview that his favorite pre-audition choice for [Batman][/f?id=10692] was [Jake Gyllenhaal][/p?id=1407], but that he was won over by [Christian Bale][/p?id=1163] after seeing his test.
  • Before the shooting began, [Christopher Nolan][/p?id=1170] invited the whole film crew to a private screening of [Blade Runner][/f?id=11038] (1982). After the film he said to the whole crew, "This is how we're going to make "[Batman][/f?id=10692]"."
  • [Christopher Nolan][/p?id=1170] wrote in the character Rachel Dawes with [Katie Holmes][/p?id=Katie%20Holmes] in mind. No other actresses were in serious consideration; it was hers to lose.
  • During earlier developments of the movie, [Ewan McGregor][/p?id=1292] was considered for the role of Scarecrow.
  • Both [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] and [Michael Keaton][/p?id=1655], from [Batman][/f?id=10692] (1989), have said they were impressed by this film.
  • [Christian Bale][/p?id=1163] revealed in interviews in 2009 that in his first ever scene with [Michael Caine][/p?id=1643] and [Morgan Freeman][/p?id=1682] (one involving [Bale][/p?id=1163] waking up in bed to find them waiting there), he fell asleep after getting ready for the scene. [Bale][/p?id=1163] described waking up to find [Michael Caine][/p?id=1643] poking him the ribs, saying "Look at that! He's bloody fallen asleep".
