Visste du?

  • [Claude Rains][/p?id=1181] at first turned down the part, feeling he would be miscast and look ridiculous as a tough New York City cop. Only after being threatened by the studio with suspension did he reluctantly accept it, but he always considered this one of his least favorite pictures.
  • Desert location shooting was so hot at times that the film melted in the camera.
  • This was a remake of The Life of Jimmy Dolan (1933). To conform with the Hays Office--the industry's censorship board, first imposed in 1934--the reporter in this film is killed by the fighter's manager. In 1933 the prizefighter, Jimmy Dolan, the central character and hero, was the killer.
  • The fourth of seven movies featuring The Dead End Kids. In the original film, The Life of Jimmy Dolan (1933), the kids are played by [Mickey Rooney][/p?id=1670] and several cast members of the our "Our Gang" comedy shorts--a much younger crowd.
  • The play opened off-broadway in New York City, New York, USA on 4 April 1933.
