Visste du?

  • [Geraldine Chaplin][/p?id=1331] plays her own real life grandmother.
  • [Robert Downey Jr.][/p?id=1809] recorded the song "Smile," which can be heard on the soundtrack from the movie. However, it was not used in the film.
  • The original cut was nearly 4 hours long; over 200 hours of footage was shot.
  • George Hayden ([Anthony Hopkins][/p?id=1053]) is the only principal character in the entire film who is fictional.
  • [John Goodman][/p?id=1485] and [Winona Ryder][/p?id=Winona%20Ryder] were attached to this project at one time.
  • [James Woods][/p?id=1426] filmed his role in one day.
  • [Richard Attenborough][/p?id=1780] cast [Marisa Tomei][/p?id=1608] in the role of [Mabel Normand][/p?id=Mabel%20Normand] after seeing her performance in the '30s set comedy Oscar (1991) on a transatlantic flight.
  • [Attenborough's][/p?id=1780] director's cut was 147 minutes, 12 minutes longer than the version shown in cinemas. [Attenborough][/p?id=1780] has said that the cuts damaged the film.
  • Originally called "Charlie", as he was known among his friends and family. Since he was more universally known as [Chaplin][/f?id=11492], that ended up being the title.
  • [Jim Carrey][/p?id=1460] was considered for the role of [Charlie Chaplin][/p?id=1145].
  • Released 15 years to the day of [Charles Chaplin's][/p?id=1145] death.
