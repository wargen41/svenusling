  • [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] paid a dentist $5,000 to make his teeth look suitably bad for the role of Max Cady. After filming, he paid $20,000 to have them fixed.
  • [Gregory Peck][/p?id=1345], who starred in [Cape Fear][/f?id=13740] (1962), appears as Cady's lawyer. [Robert Mitchum][/p?id=1815] played Max Cady in the 1962 version, and appears as Lieutenant Elgart. [Martin Balsam][/p?id=1617] played Mark Dutton in the 1962 version and the judge in this version.
  • [De Niro][/p?id=1808] researched sexual predator crimes for the part and suggested the scene where his character bites the victim.
  • The scene in the high school auditorium was totally ad-libbed by [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] and [Juliette Lewis][/p?id=Juliette%20Lewis], and done on the first take.
  • [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] was originally set to direct. He later recommended [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] for the job and personally called the director, letting him know that this was a commercial film that had potential to be a hit, which would exercise more power for Scorcese to make his films.
  • Director [Martin Scorsese's][/p?id=1619] first choice for the role of Bowden was [Harrison Ford][/p?id=1358]. He had [Robert De Niro][/p?id=1808] call [Ford][/p?id=1358] to try convincing him to take the part.
  • [Robert Redford][/p?id=1821] was also considered for the role of Sam Bowden.
  • This was [Gregory Peck's][/p?id=1345] final appearance in a theatrical film release.
  • [Drew Barrymore][/p?id=1252] tested for the role of Danielle Bowden but failed the audition. She later said she had "acted all over the place and it was just the biggest disaster of my life".
  • [Reese Witherspoon][/p?id=1772] auditioned for the role of Danielle Bowden.
  • When [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] was attached to direct he had plans on casting [Bill Murray][/p?id=1081] as Max Cady.
  • Director [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] read the original script three times while making [GoodFellas][/f?id=15927] (1990) and hated it each time because of how the Bowdens were a happy family and wanted them to be miserable.
  • [George C. Scott][/p?id=1323] was originally supposed to play the Lieutenant role. But because of health problems he dropped out a few days before filming and [Robert Mitchum][/p?id=1815] was brought in.
  • [De Niro][/p?id=1808] played scenes with [Kevin Kline][/p?id=1547] and [Phoebe Cates][/p?id=Phoebe%20Cates] in the Sam and Danielle roles for [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] when [De Niro][/p?id=1808] was trying to interest him in directing the film.
  • [Robert De Niro's][/p?id=1808] thick Max Cady accent reportedly gave [Scorsese][/p?id=1619] the creeps, and as a joke, [De Niro][/p?id=1808] would call the director's house, leaving messages as Cady.
  • [Jennifer Connelly][/p?id=1447] and [Winona Ryder][/p?id=Winona%20Ryder] both turned down the role of Danielle.
  • [Sarah Jessica Parker][/p?id=Sarah%20Jessica%20Parker] was originally cast as Danielle.
  • [Meg Ryan][/p?id=1633], [Jennifer Jason Leigh][/p?id=Jennifer%20Jason%20Leigh], [Demi Moore][/p?id=1235], [Jodie Foster][/p?id=1467], [Helen Hunt][/p?id=1368], [Bridget Fonda][/p?id=Bridget%20Fonda], [Diane Lane][/p?id=Diane%20Lane], [Brooke Shields][/p?id=Brooke%20Shields], [Nicole Kidman][/p?id=1697], [Molly Ringwald][/p?id=Molly%20Ringwald] and [Shannen Doherty][/p?id=Shannen%20Doherty] all were considered for the role of Danielle.
