  • When [Jack Benny's][/p?id=Jack%20Benny] father went to see this movie, he was outraged at the sight of his son in a Nazi uniform in the first scene and even stormed out of the theater. Jack convinced his father that it was satire, and he agreed to sit through all of it.
  • [Miriam Hopkins][/p?id=Miriam%20Hopkins] was the original choice for Maria Tura. She turned the role down because she didn't want to work with [Jack Benny][/p?id=Jack%20Benny].
