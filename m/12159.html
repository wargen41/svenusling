  • This movie came out months after [Norm MacDonald][/p?id=1699] was fired from "Saturday Night Live" (1975). When it was out in theaters, none of the shows on NBC were allowed to advertise it.
  • [Chris Farley's][/p?id=1159] last film.
  • [Howard Stern][/p?id=Howard%20Stern] was offered a cameo appearance as Satan but turned it down. [Adam Sandler][/p?id=1003] ended up with the role.
  • According to [Chevy Chase][/p?id=1152], he was impressed by the original script's raunchy, R-rated, "over the top" tone (particularly a filmed but ultimately cut gag involving [MacDonald][/p?id=1699] and Lange delivering donuts that had been photographed around their genitals) and went so far as to tell [MacDonald][/p?id=1699] and Lange to not allow any changes. However, the studio insisted on a PG-13 rating and rescheduled the film's release from February to June, where it fared poorly against blockbusters like [Godzilla (1998)][/f?id=14291]. Unfortunately, no alternate scenes had been shot and the dialogue could only be changed with the actor's re-recording their lines. This may explain why some of the dialogue is dubbed in certain scenes.
