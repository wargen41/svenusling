  • Several people involved in this film were themselves on the McCarthy-era blacklists that it is about: director [Martin Ritt][/p?id=Martin%20Ritt]; writer Walter Bernstein; and actors [Zero Mostel][/p?id=2016], [Herschel Bernardi][/p?id=Herschel%20Bernardi], [Lloyd Gough][/p?id=Lloyd%20Gough] and [Joshua Shelley][/p?id=Joshua%20Shelley].
  • The writers in the deli, according to screenwriter Walter Bernstein, were a composite of himself, Abraham Polonsky, and Arnold Manoff. All 3 writers were blacklisted by Hollywood as a result of the HUAC hearings, and all 3 co-wrote the segments for the 50's TV series You Are There.
  • This is the last on-screen film project for [Zero Mostel][/p?id=2016].
  • The scene where Hecky Brown commits suicide is based on the real suicide of blacklisted actor Philip Loeb. This information is revealed the documentary Yoo-Hoo, Mrs. Goldberg.
