Visste du?

  • [Ralph Bakshi][/p?id=Ralph%20Bakshi] originally wanted [Drew Barrymore][/p?id=1252] to play the sultry 'Holli Would' .
  • The movie was originally sold as an animated/live action horror film about a cartoonist that sleeps with a cartoon and fathers a child that can travel between both worlds.
  • The original script was scrapped and re-written without [Ralph Bakshi's][/p?id=Ralph%20Bakshi] knowledge.
  • In [Ralph Bakshi's][/p?id=Ralph%20Bakshi] original script, Holli Would was named Debbie Dallas, after the porn film Debbie Does Dallas (1978).
  • [Ralph Bakshi][/p?id=Ralph%20Bakshi] wanted [Brad Pitt][/p?id=1096] to play Jack Deebs.
  • [Traci Lords][/p?id=Traci%20Lords] was in contention for the role of Holli Would but lost out to the more widely recognized [Kim Basinger][/p?id=Kim%20Basinger].
  • Since the characters were simply created from scratch, the directors thought it to be appropriate to parody both popular Disney and Warner Bros. cartoon characters. A few examples would be the medium used for the rabbits, the old bald man and their own version of Geppetto and Pinnochio in a larger character's stomach.
