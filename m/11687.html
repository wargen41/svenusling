Visste du?

  • [Robert Downey Jr.][/p?id=1809] was set to play Jake Lonergan, but dropped out due to scheduling conflicts.
  • [Daniel Craig][/p?id=1203] was chosen because of his distinct likeness to [Steve McQueen][/p?id=1904], who starred in the cowboy epic [The Magnificent Seven][/f?id=18482].
  • [Daniel Craig][/p?id=1203] recommended [Eva Green][/p?id=Eva%20Green] for the role of Ella after working with her in [Casino Royale][/f?id=Casino%20Royale]. However, Eva turned the role down and [Olivia Wilde][/p?id=Olivia%20Wilde], who'd auditioned for Eva's Bond role of Vesper Lynd, was cast.
  • An early draft of the screenplay was written by Thomas Dean Donnelly and [Joshua Oppenheimer][/p?id=Joshua%20Oppenheimer].
  • This is DreamWorks's third comic-book adaptation, after [Road To Perdition][/f?id=17718] and [Over the Hedge][/f?id=17559].
  • The filmmakers cite [Alien][/f?id=10215] and [Predator][/f?id=17792] as an influence on the look of aliens in the film.
  • Screenwriters Roberto Orci and Alex Kurtzman described the film as "Unforgiven with aliens landing."
  • Director [Jon Favreau][/p?id=1507] was constantly harassed with demands to shoot/convert the film in 3-D, but he held his ground, claiming Westerns should only be shot on film.
  • [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] screened [The Searchers][/f?id=14153] and [Close Encounters of the Third Kind][/f?id=17212] for Roberto Orci and [Jon Favreau][/p?id=1507] so that they could get the atmosphere of the film.
  • Roberto Orci feels that the title, humorous as it may sound, will raise interest and put people off guard about the film, which will surprise them.
  • [Harrison Ford][/p?id=1358] wanted to go bareheaded in the film and not wear a hat (he is most famous for his performance in [Steven Spielberg's][/p?id=1907] Indiana Jones films, where Jones wore a fedora), but since it was a Western film he was convinced to wear a hat. In [American Graffiti][/f?id=18464] , [Harrison Ford][/p?id=1358] was asked to cut his hair (to go bareheaded with a period haircut) for the film. He refused, stating that his role was too short, and offered to wear a hat instead.
  • Once again, with the involvement of [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907], [Harrison Ford][/p?id=1358] is cast opposite a James Bond, in this case, [Daniel Craig][/p?id=1203]. The last time was when he worked opposite [Sean Connery][/p?id=1866] in [Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade][/f?id=Indiana%20Jones%20Och%20Det%20Sista%20Korstpget]. [Craig][/p?id=1203] has also appeared in 'The Adventures of Young Indiana Jones: Daredevils of the Desert (1999)'.
  • The fabric used to make Ella's dress had originally been considered for [Hailee Steinfeld's][/p?id=Hailee%20Steinfeld] wardrobe in [True Grit][/f?id=20783] but had been deemed too feminine for that movie.
