  • [Natalie Portman][/p?id=1687], who appeared in a cameo, traveled to the film's location in Jodhpur, India, to shoot for about half an hour and then spent 10 days exploring India afterward.
  • [Wes Anderson][/p?id=Wes%20Anderson] said that part of his inspiration for The [Darjeeling Limited][/f?id=11805] was [The River][/f?id=13970], and more specifically that he owes a debt of gratitude to [Martin Scorsese][/p?id=1619] for screening the film personally for him.
  • Director [Wes Anderson][/p?id=Wes%20Anderson] chose not to have an original score for his film, opting, instead, to borrow original music from Indian films including those from legendary Indian auteur Satyajit Ray's films.
  • In order to achieve a constant limp while filming, [Owen Wilson][/p?id=1714] placed a small lime in his shoe.
  • The character of Vladimir [Wolodarsky][/p?id=Wallace%20Wolodarsky] in [Wes Anderson's][/p?id=Wes%20Anderson] [The Life Aquatic With Steve Zissou][/f?id=15733], was named after [Wallace Wolodarsky][/p?id=Wallace%20Wolodarsky], who played Brendan.
  • [Adrien Brody][/p?id=1004] is the only one of the three leads to not have been in a [Wes Anderson][/p?id=Wes%20Anderson] movie prior to this. [Jason Schwartzman][/p?id=Jason%20Schwartzman] was in Rushmore while [Owen Wilson][/p?id=1714] has participated in some way to every [Anderson][/p?id=Wes%20Anderson] feature film.
  • [Wes Anderson][/p?id=Wes%20Anderson], [Roman Coppola][/p?id=Roman%20Coppola] and [Jason Schwartzman][/p?id=Jason%20Schwartzman] organically wrote the screenplay as they traveled around India together.
  • The role of Peter was written with [Adrien Brody][/p?id=1004] specifically in mind.
  • The three brothers - Francis, Peter and Jack - are actually named after [Francis Ford Coppola][/p?id=1299], [Peter Bogdanovich][/p?id=Peter%20Bogdanovich] and [Jack Nicholson][/p?id=1402].
