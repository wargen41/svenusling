Visste du?

  • The same distinctive green station wagon with wood panels was used in multiple scenes as an "automobile extra" and appears throughout the film in background shots.
  • In the original screenplay, Martin was supposed to laugh at the end, true to the film's title. This idea was abandoned, either by the director or Matthau at time of shooting.
  • According to [Bruce Dern's][/p?id=1114] autobiography, it was Matthau's idea to have [Dern][/p?id=1114], known mainly as a character actor, share top billing with him for this film - a gesture [Dern][/p?id=1114] was most grateful for.
  • The novel the film is based on is set in Stockholm, Sweden.
