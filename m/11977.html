Visste du?

  • This movie is a big-screen version of the cult cop spoof series [Police Squad!][/f?id=17473].
  • [John Houseman's][/p?id=John%20Houseman] final film appearance.
  • According to [David Zucker][/p?id=David%20Zucker] in the DVD commentary, the studio insisted on the casting of an Oscar winner in one of the major roles. This led to the casting of [George Kennedy][/p?id=1326], who had been actively campaigning for the role of Ed Hocken for months, saying that he was furious at having missed his chance to spoof himself in the movie [Airplane!][/f?id=20453].
  • [Bo Derek][/p?id=Bo%20Derek] was first choice for the role of [Jane Spencer][/p?id=Jane%20Spencer].
