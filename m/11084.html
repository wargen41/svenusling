Visste du?

  • Writer/Director/Actor [Tim Robbins][/p?id=1942] wrote a song called "Revape Amerika" for Tapeheads (1988). It was performed by [Robbins][/p?id=1942], credited as "[Bob Roberts][/f?id=11084]". [Robbins][/p?id=1942] reworked the song into "Retake America", used in this film.
  • All the songs were written by [Tim Robbins][/p?id=1942] and [David Robbins][/p?id=David%20Robb], his brother. [Tim Robbins][/p?id=1942] not only sings in the film, but he also plays the harmonica. [Jack Black][/p?id=1397] sings background vocals on the soundtrack.
  • When [Tim Robbins][/p?id=1942] approached his actor friends about doing the movie, he said most of them asked to play newscasters.
  • [Jack Black's][/p?id=1397] feature film debut.
  • When [Tim Robbins][/p?id=1942] directed his first feature film, [Bob Roberts][/f?id=11084] (1992), [Helen Hunt][/p?id=1368] appeared in it in a brief cameo as a television news reporter. When [Hunt][/p?id=1368] made her feature directorial debut with [Then She Found Me][/f?id=20337] (2007), [Robbins][/p?id=1942] returned the favor and appeared briefly as one of the interviewees on the [Bette Midler][/p?id=Bette%20Midler] character's talk show.
  • The idea for the film originated from a sketch on "Saturday Night Live" (1975).
