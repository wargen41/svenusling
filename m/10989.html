Visste du?

  • Originally, [Alex Winter][/p?id=Alex%20Winter] auditioned for the role of Ted and [Keanu Reeves][/p?id=1536] auditioned for the role of Bill.
  • In the original script, the time machine was a 1969 Chevy Van, but the filmmakers thought that it would be a rip-off of [Back to the Future][/f?id=20376] (1985). So, they changed it to a phone booth (apparently unconcerned that "[Doctor Who][/f?id=12192]" (1963) uses a police telephone box as its time machine). Also, when they used the van, Bill and Ted picked up more historical figures than they did in the final film.
