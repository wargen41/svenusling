Visste du?

  • Filmed in just five days, using the same set and much of the same cast as [Smoke][/f?id=Smoke] (1995). The premise of the film came to directors [Wayne Wang][/p?id=Wayne%20Wang] and Paul Auster while watching an improvisation session between [Harvey Keitel][/p?id=1364] et. al. to help them get into character for filming [Smoke][/f?id=Smoke] (1995). They decided that the improvisations were so funny that they would spend a few days after shooting [Smoke][/f?id=Smoke] just filming film is almost entirely improvised. Auster and [Wang][/p?id=Wayne%20Wang] claim to have "borrowed" the idea of shooting another movie on the back of an existing one from [Roger Corman][/p?id=Roger%20Corman] who often used to shoot movies very quickly on leftover sets from other productions.
  • [Jim Jarmusch's][/p?id=Jim%20Jarmusch] scene was originally intended for [William Hurt][/p?id=2006] who was to reprise his role from [Smoke][/f?id=Smoke] (1995). But [Hurt][/p?id=2006] was unavailable.
