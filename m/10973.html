Visste du?

  • [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907] was attached to this project and wanted [Jack Nicholson][/p?id=1402] to star, but dropped out due to his full schedule, soon after which [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] signed on.
  • [Ewan McGregor][/p?id=1292] was cast as Young Ed Bloom when producers noticed the striking similarity between him and pictures of a young [Albert Finney][/p?id=1015], who plays Senior Ed Bloom.
  • Young Edward becomes a traveling salesman for a company that sells hands with metal tools as fingers, all held together by a plastic base. The crew were aware that people would draw connections between it and [Edward Scissorhands][/f?id=13319] (1990), and therefore purposely did not include scissors in the design.
  • French actress [Marion Cotillard's][/p?id=Marion%20Cotillard] first American feature film.
