Visste du?

  • Stephen J. Rivele's and Christopher Wilkinson's first draft sprawled across 200 pages, taking Ali from childhood up to the present.
  • [Will Smith][/p?id=2002] gained 35 pounds to match Ali's 220.
  • Shortly after Ali knocks out George Foreman, there is a close-up of Ali with what appears to be a lone white butterfly flying behind him. This is no doubt an allusion to 'Float like a butterfly, Sting like a bee'.
  • Both [Will Smith][/p?id=2002] and [Michael Mann][/p?id=1658] offered to put up their salaries in case the film went over-budget.
  • As the project lay in "development hell" for more than a decade, several directors attempted to make the film until [Michael Mann][/p?id=1658] was finally chosen. The list included: [Oliver Stone][/p?id=1705], [Spike Lee][/p?id=1889], and [Norman Jewison][/p?id=Norman%20Jewison].
  • [Michael Mann][/p?id=1658] and [Will Smith][/p?id=2002] always wanted [Jon Voight][/p?id=1510] for the role of Howard Cosell.
  • In the edited TV version, director [Michael Mann][/p?id=1658] is credited instead as "[Alan Smith][/p?id=Alan%20Smith]", an obvious variant of [Alan Smithee][/p?id=Alan%20Smithee].
