Visste du?

  • Paramount Pictures originally wanted [Robert Redford][/p?id=1821] to play the part of Charlie.
  • The ending was changed to leave open the possibility of a sequel.
  • In a 2003 UK movie survey, Charlie Croker's ([Michael Caine][/p?id=1643]) line, "You're only supposed to blow the bloody doors off!" was voted the most memorable line in any film.
  • Screenwriter Troy Kennedy-Martin wanted [Nicol Williamson][/p?id=1695] for the role of Mr. Bridger, a character he envisioned as "tough as nails" and totally in control of the situation. Director [Peter Collinson][/p?id=Peter%20Collinson] offered the role to [Noel Coward][/p?id=Noel%20Coward] instead, which changed the tone of the character.
  • [Peter Yates][/p?id=Peter%20Yates] was first offered the job of director.
  • Parts of the stock film footage of this film were later used in "[MacGyver][/f?id=15874]: Thief of Budapest (#1.3)" (1985). In the story, Macgyver and a band of gypsies use the Mini Coopers to escape from Hungary.
