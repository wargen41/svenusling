Visste du?

  • George Banks says that he doesn't want the wedding to bankrupt him so he'll end up wandering the streets in a bathrobe. This happens to [Steve Martin's][/p?id=1903] character in [The Jerk][/f?id=19961] (1979).
  • [Steve Martin's][/p?id=1903] character of George Banks was named after [David Tomlinson's][/p?id=David%20Tom] character of the same name in [Mary Poppins][/f?id=16056] (1964).
  • Actor [Eugene Levy][/p?id=1291] appears in both this movie and [Father of the Bride Part II][/f?id=11336] (1995), but as different characters in each one.
  • George's middle name, Stanley, is named after [Spencer Tracy's][/p?id=1888] character in the original [Father of the Bride][/f?id=11334] (1950).
  • Among the wedding gifts Annie receives is a Venus de Milo statue with a clock in the stomach. In the original [Father of the Bride][/f?id=11334] (1950), Kay also receives this as one of her wedding gifts. Both look at it with disdain.
  • [Phoebe Cates][/p?id=Phoebe%20Cates] was offered the role of Annie Banks, but had to decline it because of her pregnancy.
  • [Tom][/p?id=[David%20Tom] Irish][/p?id=David%2520Tom%5D%20Irish] made his film debut in the 1950 version, [Father of the Bride][/f?id=11334] (1950).
  • [Tom][/p?id=[David%20Tom] Irish][/p?id=David%2520Tom%5D%20Irish] plays the same character, Ben Banks, in both the [1950][/f?id=11334] and the 1991 versions of "Father of the Bride".
