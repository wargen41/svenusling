Visste du?

  • The shot in which the camera moves in on the mother discovering that Nathan Jr. is gone is a direct homage to [The Evil Dead][/f?id=13513] (1981), a film on which [Joel Coen][/p?id=Joel%20Coen] was an editor.
  • The alarm button the clerk presses in the convenience store reads "Odegaard-Trend Security." This is the name of the security company in [Sam Raimi's][/p?id=Sam%20Raimi] [Crimewave][/f?id=Ring%20-%20S%C3%A5%20M%C3%B6rdar%20Vi] (1985).
  • The character Leonard Smalls is a reference to Lenny Small from "Of Mice and Men."
  • H.I. can be seen wearing a uniform with the name "Hudsucker Industries" written on it. This is also the name of the company in [The Hudsucker Proxy][/f?id=Strebern] (1994) also by the [Coen][/p?id=Joel%20Coen] brothers.
  • [Kevin Costner][/p?id=1544] turned down the lead role.
  • The [Coen][/p?id=Joel%20Coen] brothers wrote [Holly Hunter's][/p?id=1375] character specifically for her.
