  • [Claire Forlani][/p?id=Claire%20Forlani] was cast as Trillian St. James. She even started shooting, but walked out after just three days, due to creative differences with director [Stephen Sommers][/p?id=Stephen%20Sommers]. The part subsequently went to [Famke Janssen][/p?id=Famke%20Janssen].
