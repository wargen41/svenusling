  • In June, 2001 Newsweek revealed that print ads for at least four movies released by Columbia Pictures, including this one and [A Knight's Tale][/f?id=13412] (2001) contained glowing comments from a film reviewer who did not exist. The fake critic, "David Manning," was actually a friend by the same name in Connecticut who had agreed to let his Columbia ad-marketing friend use his name. Manning was misrepresented as a reviewer for a newspaper in the small Connecticut town of Ridgefield.
