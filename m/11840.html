  • Director [Scott Derrickson][/p?id=Scott%20Derrickson] was an ardent admirer of sci-film director [Robert Wise][/p?id=Robert%20Wise], and had wanted to remake [Wise's][/p?id=Robert%20Wise] [The Day the Earth Stood Still][/f?id=16007] (1951) since 1993.
  • [Jennifer Connelly][/p?id=1447] was [Scott Derrickson's][/p?id=Scott%20Derrickson] first choice for the role of Helen Benson.
  • According to [Keanu Reeves][/p?id=1536], in [The Day the Earth Stood Still][/f?id=16007] (1951) Klaatu was an alien WITH a human body, while in this film Klaatu is an alien IN a human body.
  • Klaatu uses the phrase "tipping point", which is a climatological term meaning the point at which the reversal of climate change becomes impossible. Some scientists believe that this has already happened.
  • The words "Klaatu Barada Nikto", which are in the original 1951 film spoken by Helen Benson to Gort the robot, has featured in many other productions, for instance in [Army Of Darkness][/f?id=Army%20Of%20Darkness] (1992), where they are the spell Ash must speak to stop the undead army from appearing.
  • According to the filmmakers, [John Cleese][/p?id=1479] was the most difficult choice in casting as he was primarily noted for comic roles. [Cleese][/p?id=1479] felt that at his age, a dramatic role with subtle humour would be an easier role to play rather than another manic old man.
  • The filmmakers thoroughly enjoyed working with [Cleese][/p?id=1479], and were sorry when shooting was finished.
