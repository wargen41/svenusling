  • Actors [Ken Foree][/p?id=Ken%20Foree], [Scott H. Reiniger][/p?id=Scott%20H.%20Reiniger] and [Tom Savini][/p?id=Tom%20Savini] all appeared in the original 1978 version of this film, but playing different characters. [Ken Foree][/p?id=Ken%20Foree] delivers the tagline he delivered as "Peter" from the 1978 version of Dawn of the Dead; "When there's no more room in Hell, the dead will walk the Earth."
  • Director [Zack Snyder][/p?id=Zack%20Snyder] personally chose most of the music used in the film. His choices included "[The Man][/f?id=15962] Comes Around" by [Johnny Cash][/p?id=Johnny%20Cash], and Richard Cheese's cover of Disturbed's "Down With the Sickness". [Snyder][/p?id=Zack%20Snyder] was the only person who thought these songs should be in the film, as most of the producers were against it.
  • For the scene where Ana stitches Kenneth's wounds, the director hired a real nurse for the close-ups. She misunderstood the director's directions to go deeper and inadvertently punctured [Ving Rhames'][/p?id=1979] skin and stitched the prosthesis to his arm. He didn't say anything until after the scene was done filming and the director thought the blood was merely "a really good effect".
  • [Zack Snyder's][/p?id=Zack%20Snyder] directorial debut.
  • [Nathan Fillion][/p?id=1688] auditioned for the role of Michael.
