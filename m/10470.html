Visste du?

  • [Arthur][/f?id=10470] states his father died at the age of 44 in homage to [Steve Gordon][/p?id=Steve%20Gordon] who directed the original [Arthur][/f?id=10470] (1981) and also died at the age of 44.
  • [Ricky Gervais][/p?id=1796] turned down the lead role. He told the press, "I said 'no' straight-away. Why would I mess with a perfect comedy? They know people don't watch films that have the wrong font - it's got an '80s font".
  • [Kevin Smith][/p?id=1549] was approached to direct at one point.
  • The '[Arthur'][/f?id=10470] films are considered a modern reworking of the P.G. Wodehouse's Jeeves and Wooster tales, [Arthur][/f?id=10470] is the equivalent of Bertie Wooster and his servant Hobson the equivalent of Jeeves.
  • Theatrical film debut of director [Jason Winer][/p?id=Jason%20Winer].
