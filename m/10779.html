Visste du?

  • [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] originally wanted [Sammy Davis Jr.][/p?id=Sammy%20Davis%20Jr.], a favorite star of his since childhood, to play the role of Betelgeuse but studio executives didn't like that idea at all.
  • During the sequence where Adam and Barbara enter Juno's office and see her speaking to a recently deceased football team, a movie theater full of ghosts can be seen through Juno's office window. When the film was first released in theaters, the scene created the illusion that the audience were themselves being watched by the ghosts. Among the ghosts in the audience are a red skeleton and a green skeleton (identical to the ones seen in [Tim Burton's][/p?id=1937] later movie, [Mars Attacks!][/f?id=16050] (1996)), a woman with red hair, and two men in suits and Ray-Ban style sunglasses.
  • [Michael Keaton][/p?id=1655] spent only two weeks filming his part in the film, which lasts 17.5 minutes out of the 92-minute running time. It is [Keaton's][/p?id=1655] favorite film of his own.
  • The title character of Beetle Juice (1988) is named for a bright red star in the constellation of Orion, Betelgeuse. The studio disliked the title and wanted to call the film "House Ghosts". As a joke, [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] suggested the name "Scared Sheetless" and was horrified when the studio actually considered using it.
  • The only cast member who would initially commit to the project was [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis]. [Michael Keaton][/p?id=1655], [Winona Ryder][/p?id=Winona%20Ryder], [Catherine O'Hara][/p?id=1138], and [Sylvia Sidney][/p?id=Sylvia%20Sidney] all said no at least once. Producer David Geffen convinced [Michael Keaton's][/p?id=1655] manager to convince Michael to meet with director [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937]. Once Michael said yes, [Tim Burton][/p?id=1937] personally called [Sylvia Sidney][/p?id=Sylvia%20Sidney] and begged her to do the movie, and he flew out to meet with [Catherine O'Hara][/p?id=1138] to convince her as well.
  • [Juliette Lewis][/p?id=Juliette%20Lewis] auditioned for the role of Lydia. [Lori Loughlin][/p?id=Lori%20Loughlin], [Diane Lane][/p?id=Diane%20Lane], [Sarah Jessica Parker][/p?id=Sarah%20Jessica%20Parker], [Brooke Shields][/p?id=Brooke%20Shields], [Justine Bateman][/p?id=Justine%20Bateman], [Molly Ringwald][/p?id=Molly%20Ringwald] and [Jennifer Connelly][/p?id=1447] all turned down the same role.
