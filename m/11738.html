Visste du?

  • Originally scheduled to star [Michael Douglas][/p?id=1646], who pulled out claiming that [Geena Davis'][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] role was expanded at his character's expense.
  • [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] did most of her own stunts for the film. [Renny Harlin][/p?id=1777] is famous for pushing the actual actors to do their own stunts whenever possible. While promoting the film, [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] appeared on talk shows with clips of her doing the stunts over and over (including one take where she fell out the window too soon, rolled down the roof and under the carriage) and explaining the bruises and injuries she sustained while filming.
  • Bankrupted Carolco Pictures, the production company behind the movie.
  • The "impossible" stunt where [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] appears to jump out of a window, roll on the roof of a building and then land on a moving carriage was actually done by digitally compositing two separate shots: one of her falling, and another of her already in the carriage, pretending she's just landed there.
  • [Keanu Reeves][/p?id=1536], [Liam Neeson][/p?id=1584], [Jeff Bridges][/p?id=1441], [Ralph Fiennes][/p?id=1767], [Charlie Sheen][/p?id=1149] and [Michael Keaton][/p?id=1655] all reportedly turned down the male lead role.
  • [Oliver Reed][/p?id=1704] was originally cast as Mordechai Fingers but was fired from the production after becoming involved in a bar fight and mooning at [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis]. He was replaced by [George Murcell][/p?id=George%20Murcell].
  • [Renny Harlin][/p?id=1777] was dating [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] at the time and convinced Mario Kassar that [Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] should take the leading role. [Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] at the time was only known for light comedies, and [Harlin][/p?id=1777] was sure this would turn her into an action-adventure star.
  • [Michael Douglas][/p?id=1646] originally agreed to do the film on two conditions. First, the filming had to start immediately because he was only available for a limited time. Second, his character had to have the same amount of screen time as [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis].
  • Total cost: $115 million. Total box office earnings: $10 million. Total loss: $105 million.
  • [Renny Harlin][/p?id=1777] was so preoccupied with trying to find a male lead after [Michael Douglas][/p?id=1646] quit that the set construction and script work was done without his input. When shooting was set to begin, he didn't like what he saw, so massive rebuilding and rewriting had to be done, at increased expense.
  • After [Michael Douglas][/p?id=1646] quit, [Geena Davis][/p?id=Geena%20Davis] wanted to leave too. However, she was contractually obligated to finish the film.
  • Shot at the Mediterranean Studios in Malta, the same studio where Raise the [Titanic][/f?id=20448] (1980), [Popeye][/f?id=15396] (1980) and [Orca][/f?id=Orca%20-%20Djupets%20H%C3%A4mnare] (1977) were also shot, all - like [Cutthroat Island][/f?id=11738] (1995) - box office bombs.
  • [Matthew Modine][/p?id=Matthew%20Modine] was partly cast because he is an experienced fencer.
