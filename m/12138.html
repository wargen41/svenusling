  • Originally titled "Simon Says" (where Zeus was scripted as a woman) and was considered as the third sequel to [Lethal Weapon][/f?id=13287] (1987).
  • The sandwich board that [Bruce Willis][/p?id=1117] wore while filming in Harlem said "I hate everyone" rather than the more racist version that appears in the film. The sign was changed with CGI in post-production to read "I Hate Niggers", though the "I hate everyone" version is used for some television broadcasts of the film.
  • The sex scene between [Jeremy Irons][/p?id=1451] and [Sam Phillips][/p?id=Sam%20Phillips] was added in at the last minute because [John McTiernan][/p?id=John%20McTiernan] knew that the film would get an R rating and he might as well put a sex scene in.
  • Although he wasn't hired for the film, [Alan Rickman][/p?id=1012] is still credited as playing Hans Gruber (in McClane's flashback).
  • [Laurence Fishburne][/p?id=1567] was the original choice to play Zeus Carver, but turned down the part. When he reconsidered the decision, [Samuel L. Jackson][/p?id=1856] was already cast.
  • [Jeremy Irons][/p?id=1451] replaced [David Thewlis][/p?id=1227].
  • [Sean Connery][/p?id=1866] was [John McTiernan's][/p?id=John%20McTiernan] very first choice for the role of Simon Gruber. He turned down the role, saying that he didn't want to play such a diabolical villain.
  • Although [Bruce Willis][/p?id=1117] and [Samuel L. Jackson][/p?id=1856] had both appeared in [Loaded Weapon 1][/f?id=15607] (1993) and [Pulp Fiction][/f?id=17549] (1994), this is the first movie where the two appear on-screen together.
  • The only "[Die Hard][/f?id=12136]" movie based on an original screenplay. The first [Die Hard][/f?id=12136] (1988) is based on the novel by 'Roderick Thorpe', [Die Hard 2][/f?id=12137] (1990) is based on a novel by Walter Wager and [Die Hard 4.0][/f?id=12139] (2007) is based on an article by [John Carlin][/p?id=John%20Carlin].
