Visste du?

  • [Gael García Bernal][/p?id=Gael%20Garc%C3%ADa%20Bernal] was the first choice for the role played by [Unax Ugalde][/p?id=Unax%20Ugalde].
  • [Leonor Watling][/p?id=Leonor%20Watling] was offered a role but turned it down.
  • Several years before the film was almost made with [Antonio Banderas][/p?id=1059] set to direct and star with a script by Enrique Urbizu.
  • At a cost of 24 million Euros, this is the most expensive Spanish film ever made. Director [Agustín Díaz Yanes][/p?id=Agust%C3%ADn%20D%C3%ADaz%20Yanes] called that amount enough for 'a European super-production and an American rubbish-production'.
  • The role of Inquisitor Emilio Bocanegra is played by a woman, veteran actress [Blanca Portillo][/p?id=Blanca%20Portillo]. This caused controversy, not so much because it might look disrespectful towards the Church, but because many fans could not see the point in doing something like this. [Díaz Yanes][/p?id=Agust%C3%ADn%20D%C3%ADaz%20Yanes] said that he had had the idea of using a female actress from the beginning, because he saw 'that Great Inquisitor role' as something too cartoonish and black-and-white and wanted to introduce a fresh, less recognizable element to it, and still make viewers suitably uneasy about so powerful and fearful a character. He cited the Oscar-winning supporting role of [Linda Hunt][/p?id=1586] in [The Year of Living Dangerously][/f?id=11339] (1982), where she played a man, as an example of what he wanted to achieve. Incidentally, [Blanca Portillo][/p?id=Blanca%20Portillo] wears her hair very short in her next film, Volver (2006/I), as a result of having had it cut for this role.
