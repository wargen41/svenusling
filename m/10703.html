Visste du?

  • [Jeremy Renner][/p?id=1452] was cast as Alex Hopper, but dropped out in order to co-star in The Master, which he also later dropped out of.
  • The movie is based on the Milton Bradley game "[Battleship][/f?id=10703]" that has been manufactured since 1931. The original paper and pencil version of the game predates World War I.
  • Some of the artillery used in the film is shaped like the pegs used in the game.
  • [Teresa Palmer][/p?id=1924] and [Rosie Huntington-Whiteley][/p?id=Rosie%20Huntington-Whiteley] auditioned for the role of Sam.
  • Singer Rihanna's acting debut.
  • The battleship Missouri was the key plot of the movie Under Siege, where it was about to be decommissioned. In [Battleship][/f?id=10703], this boat is set as a museum.
  • Retired Army LT. Col Mick Canales is played by real life active duty Army Colonel Gregory Gadson. In March 2012 Gadson became Garrison Commander of Fort Belvoir just south of Washington D.C. in Northern Virginia. Col. Gadson is the first bilateral amputee to serve as a Garrison Commander to any post in the United States Army.
