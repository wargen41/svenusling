Visste du?

  • The board in the Scrabble game has several words relating to pregnancy and childbirth. In particular, one of the players somehow managed to play "episiotomy."
  • Although this sequel to [Father of the Bride][/f?id=11335] (1991) is not a remake of [Father's Little Dividend][/f?id=16434] (1951), (the sequel to the original [Father of the Bride][/f?id=11334]), the opening scenes of the two films, including the narration spoken to camera by the father character, are virtually identical.
  • When [Diane Keaton][/p?id=1242] was given the script for the movie, she nearly refused to film when she found out she had to pretend to be pregnant as she was nearly 50. It took a bag of humbugs and a lot of pleading from [Steve Martin][/p?id=1903] and [Nancy Meyers][/p?id=Nancy%20Meyers] to get her to join.
  • The set used for the interior of the Banks' home had to be rebuilt from scratch for the sequel. With no presumption of a sequel during production of the original movie, the set was destroyed after production completed. Set crew had to recreate the entirety of the set based only on a few remnant sketches of the original set, and had to infer most measurements based on the known sizes of various reference items in the original film.
