Visste du?

  • [Antoine Fuqua][/p?id=Antoine%20Fuqua] was originally set to direct this project in 2004 with [Denzel Washington][/p?id=1240] and [Benicio Del Toro][/p?id=Benicio%20Del%20Toro] starring, but production was halted one month before shooting after Universal Pictures canceled the film over budget concerns.
  • [Russell Crowe][/p?id=1846] requested tape recordings of Richie Roberts speaking in order to match his voice mannerisms accurately.
  • Director [Ridley Scott][/p?id=1797] had read the first draft of [Steven Zaillian's][/p?id=Steven%20Zaillian] screenplay before filming Kingdom of Heaven (2005) and instantly became interested in directing it. While filming his next feature, A Good Year (2006), [Scott][/p?id=1797] and [Russell Crowe][/p?id=1846] extensively discussed the project, which ultimately led to them signing on.
  • [Russell Crowe][/p?id=1846] and [Brad Pitt][/p?id=1096] were director [Ridley Scott][/p?id=1797] and producer Brian Grazer's first two choices to portray Richie Roberts. [Crowe][/p?id=1846] was ultimately cast in the role.
  • [James Gandolfini][/p?id=1420] was offered the role of Detective Trupo, but turned it down.
  • In the much-forgotten sci-fi action thriller, [Virtuosity][/f?id=21338] (1995), [Denzel Washington][/p?id=1240] starred as a heroic policeman while a then-unknown [Russell Crowe][/p?id=1846] was cast as the villain.
