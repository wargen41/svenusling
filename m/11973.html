  • Based on the true story of William Lishman's and Joseph Duff's experiments on migrating birds. Lishman and Duff provided actual "imprinted" birds for the making of the film, as well as the actual aircraft used.
  • Release of the film was delayed after a seven-year old girl, Jessica Dubroff, was killed at the controls of a small plane that crashed amidst a much-publicized transcontinental flight attempt, along with her father and flight instructor.
  • Nine years after they played father and daughter in Fly Away Home, [Jeff Daniels][/p?id=1442] and [Anna Paquin][/p?id=1047] filmed a sex scene together for [The Squid And The Whale][/f?id=The%20Squid%20And%20The%20Whale]. [Daniels][/p?id=1442] said in interview at the time that the way they coped with filming the sex scene was by trying "not to think about...you know, geese."
  • In the film, Amy (played by [Anna Paquin][/p?id=1047]) was raised in New Zealand and then moved to Canada after her mother's death. In real life, [Anna Paquin][/p?id=1047] was born in Canada and raised in New Zealand.
