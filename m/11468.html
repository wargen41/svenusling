Visste du?

  • [Gore Verbinski][/p?id=Gore%20Verbinski] pushed production back a few months because [Leonardo DiCaprio][/p?id=1582] had to re-shoot scenes for [Gangs Of New York][/f?id=14214] (2002), The delay led to [James Gandolfini's][/p?id=1420] withdrawal, because he had to go back to work on "The Sopranos" (1999). He was replaced by [Tom Hanks][/p?id=1950]. [David Fincher][/p?id=1215], [Cameron Crowe][/p?id=Cameron%20Crowe] and [Lasse Hallström][/p?id=Lasse%20Hallstr%C3%B6m] were asked to direct before [Steven Spielberg][/p?id=1907], who only wanted to produce the movie, took over.
  • When it appeared that [Gore Verbinski][/p?id=Gore%20Verbinski] would direct, [Chloë Sevigny][/p?id=1155] and [Ed Harris][/p?id=1267] were both considered for roles.
  • Until he saw the results of [Leonardo DiCaprio's][/p?id=1582] work, the real [Frank Abagnale Jr.][/p?id=Frank%20Abagnale%20Jr.] didn't think [DiCaprio][/p?id=1582] was "suave" enough to play him.
  • [Jennifer Garner][/p?id=Jennifer%20Garner] shot her scenes in one day.
  • The concluding part of [Steven Spielberg's][/p?id=1907] unofficial "running man" trilogy, which started with [Artificial Intelligence: AI][/f?id=10099] (2001) and continued with [Minority Report][/f?id=16200] (2002).
  • The first cut of the film was about 80% authentic, as quoted at the epilogue of Abagnale's book. Some scenes were corrected, added and change as per request of the real [Frank Abagnale Jr.][/p?id=Frank%20Abagnale%20Jr.] to ensure total authenticity.
  • [Steven Spielberg's][/p?id=1907] original choice for the role of Frank Abigale Jr. was [Johnny Depp][/p?id=1505].
