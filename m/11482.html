Visste du?

  • Based on the true story of the Wineville Chicken Coop Murders, also known as the Wineville Chicken Murders.
  • Both [Hilary Swank][/p?id=1374] and [Reese Witherspoon][/p?id=1772] lobbied for the role of Christine Collins. [Clint Eastwood][/p?id=1184] cast [Angelina Jolie][/p?id=1043] at the suggestion of Executive Producer [Ron Howard][/p?id=Ron%20Howard], who noted that [Jolie's][/p?id=1043] "look" would fit best with the time period.
  • [Ron Howard][/p?id=Ron%20Howard] planned to direct the film himself, until scheduling conflicts dictated otherwise. He then decided to serve as Executive Producer.
  • [Clint Eastwood][/p?id=1184] agreed to direct the same afternoon he read the script.
  • Twice in the film, [Angelina Jolie's][/p?id=1043] character "Christine" says: "Never start a fight, but always finish it". This is the same quote that Captain John Sheridan says his father always told him in the 90's sci-fi series "[Babylon 5][/f?id=10531]" (1994) which writer [J. Michael Straczynski][/p?id=J.%20Michael%20Straczynski] also wrote. Likewise, Attorney Hahn's description of how his daughter's absence continues to affect him daily is nearly identical to the way Sheridan describes grappling with his wife's death.
