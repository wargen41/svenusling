  • The real Stage Door Canteen on 44th Street was unable to be used for filming, as it was still an operating nightclub during World War II. As such, for this movie, it was re-created at R.K.O. Radio Pictures Studios in Culver City, California.
  • Features the only movie appearance of Katharine Cornell (Herself).
  • [Katharine Hepburn's][/p?id=1531] only musical movie.
  • Along with [Too Many Kisses][/f?id=Too%20Many%20Kisses] (1925), this is one of only two movies in which [Harpo Marx][/p?id=Harpo%20Marx] appeared without his brothers Chico and Groucho.
  • A similar movie, [Hollywood Canteen][/f?id=Hollywood%20Canteen] (1944), followed this movie. While it had the same basic concept, it was not a sequel to this movie.
  • [Loretta Young][/p?id=Loretta%20Young], [Danny Kaye][/p?id=Danny%20Kaye], and Morton Downey were announced to make cameo appearances in this movie, but eventually did not appear.
