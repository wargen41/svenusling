Visste du?

  • Shortly before his death in 2014, director [Mike Nichols][/p?id=Mike%20Nichols] revealed that out of all the films he had directed in his lifetime, he considered this to be his magnum opus.
  • [Jeffrey Wright][/p?id=1445] was the only member of the original Broadway cast to appear in the film version.
  • Sir [Michael Gambon][/p?id=1649] reportedly threatened violence on anyone on set who addressed him by his full proper title.
  • Both [Robert Altman][/p?id=1804] and Neil LaBute were considered to direct. It was [Altman][/p?id=1804] who first attracted [Al Pacino][/p?id=1008] to the role of Roy Cohn.
  • Some of the actors play multiple parts, just as was done in the play.
  • [Jodie Foster][/p?id=1467] was considered for the role of Nurse Emily/The Angel America.
  • [Dustin Hoffman][/p?id=1254] was considered for the role of [Roy M. Cohn][/p?id=Roy%20M.%20Cohn].
